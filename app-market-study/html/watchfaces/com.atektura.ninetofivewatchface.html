Description

What time do you start working, and how long does it take to leave it? Now you name it! 9to5 can become whatever: 8to4, 7to3, or even 12to13. It's all your choice!

9to5 Watch Face will let you know, how much time left to leave your work.

3.0 version of 9to5 Watch Face is here!

- "Start hour", "Work length" and "Lunch break" with 5-minute-precision time planning,
- working days,
- analog/digital clock switch (with both 12 and 24h time formats),
- date and battery status switches,
- info switch to enable/disable text counter,
- orange "time to get to work" indicator,
- dedicated smartphone settings app with live preview.
- smartphone clock widget!
- 8 color schemes,
- battery saving seconds tick/smooth modes,
- night mode.
