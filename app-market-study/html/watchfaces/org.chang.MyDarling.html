Description

MyDarling is an application for couples.
After registering your lover or friend, you can check the birthday and anniversaries and use many useful features.

[Main Features]
1. The birthday and anniversaries are shown on widget. the form of widget are 4*3, 4*2, 2*1, 1*1.
2. On 4*3 widget, the character's face will be changed according to the elapsed time of the received calls,messages.
3. New widget theme will be updated periodically.
4. The time of incoming calls and outgoing calls are shown by graph.
5. The mainly anniversaries(like 500th day, 1000th day) are shown.
6. For writing the memory with your lover, the diary is available.
7. The biorhythm of lover can be checked.
8. Support for Android Wear.
