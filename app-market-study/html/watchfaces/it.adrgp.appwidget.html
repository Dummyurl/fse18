Description

Looking for an AppWidget clock simple, clear and essential ?
Personal Watch is a AppWidget that shows the date with a touch of style.
Once installed on your home you can configure and customize it to your personal taste with a finger tap.
If you own an Android Wear swartwatch you can run it as an interactive Watch Face.

Features:

- Every aspect of the watch face can be configured with various options:

3 watch face shapes;
52 different vector textures;
95 colors for the LED;
4 types of LED lighting;
6 different borders;
12 character digits fonts;
transparency;
screen glare.

- Multicolor LED changing on time of day or every hour;
- Time and Calendar: tint changing on time of the day or fully customizable (light/dark);
- Tapping an AppWidget/Android Wear Watch Face, you can set alarm clock and timer, view calendar events;
- Supports Android Wear smart watch devices;
- Right-To-Left language support (eg Arabic, Hebrew);
- Great Battery save: AppWidgets are updated only when device screen is on;
- AppWidget sizes: (1x1), icon sized; (2x2) and (4x1), for general use; (3x3), good for tablets;
- Supports any screen size/density, all locale languages, time zone changes and manual time set.

Additional notes:

- Android Permission required:

"Prevent tablet/phone from sleeping": just to guarantee that the component which detects if the display is on has been initialized correctly. Personal Watch will not update EVER AppWidgets when the screen is off;
"set an alarm": to set the alarm clock and the timer tapping the AppWidget or the watch face on the smart watch.

- This application was developed and tested on Nexus 4 and Nexus 7 (2012) devices with original firmware by Google. If you notice some malfunctions in this application please report it via email, quoting model and producer.
- It is not guaranteed to work on devices with third party firmware which requires "root" installation procedures.
- When you use applications with functionality of "Task Killers", be sure to exclude Personal Watch from the list of tasks to be removed.
- If the Personal Watch process is terminated by the system/task killer/user all AppWidgets will no longer be updated. In this regard has been inserted the ability to restart the application, clicking any of the AppWidget installed.
