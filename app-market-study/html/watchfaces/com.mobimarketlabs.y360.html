Description

Watch Face Y360 - (YELLOW 360dp) for most modern smartwatch models: moto360 2 gen. and other.
But, worked fine with other new and older smartwatchs.

1. This watch face with steps counter and battery level.
2. Built-in Calendar.

- Русский
