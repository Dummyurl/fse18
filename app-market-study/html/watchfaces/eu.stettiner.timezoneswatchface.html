Description

--- NOW, available also as a Phone Widget and Live Wallpaper! ---

Two time zones of your choice displayed together on a minimalistic watch face.
Current weather and date presentation.

Do you communicate with people living in different time zones? "Time Zones" Watch Face is for you!

Your watch will display, both, your current time and the second location's time (by selecting city's name on your phone).

The animating sun indicates day time of the specific location and the moon stands for night time.
Now you will never bother anyone in the middle of the night - especially your own boss!

Experience 3D effects moving your wrist (Sun / Moon), due to the use of an in-built gyroscope.

Features:
- current weather presentation (please note, you must choose the "default timezone" in app's settings on your phone to activate weather),
- date presentation - for both timezones,
- ambient mode,
- animated moon / sun (gyroscope controlled),
- alternative moon / sun indicator - flat design,
- settings,
- 12 / 24h modes,
- works also as a phone widget and live wallpaper.

Designed for Android Wear.
Supported smartwatches: LG G Watch R, LG G Watch, Motorola Moto 360, Sony SmartWatch 3, Asus ZenWatch, Samsung Gear Live.
