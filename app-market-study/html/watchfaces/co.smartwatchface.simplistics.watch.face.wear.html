Description

Pure is a free watch face, which looks great on the Moto 360 and other smartwatches. You can choose between the analog or the digital watch face.

This watch face is for rectangular smartwatches such as the Samsung Gear Live and round watches such as the Moto 360.

----

Key custom features:

o Digital Watch Face
o Analog Watch Face
o Puristic design
o Date

----

How to install Pure:

o Download the free app on Google Play
o Open it on your mobile
o Tap & hold on the watch face on the Android Wear device
o Select one of the two Pure watch faces

In case the watch face is not present, please go to your Android Wear app on the smartphone and in settings press on synchronize apps.

Weather data is based on http://openweathermap.org

----

Visit our exclusive wearface collection for Android Wear on the Play Store http://goo.gl/QOsOu4. Also please let us know if you like to have any other watch faces to be added to our selection of watch faces.
