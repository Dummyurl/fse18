Description

Presenting "1000+ Animated Watch Faces” that features a supreme collection of 1000+ breath-taking animated watch faces with best in class user interface.
The carefully crafted design of the app syncs up your mobile and your smart watch seamlessly and provides rich user experience making it simple and easy to use.

The unique features of the app are:

- Analog and Digital Dials

- Option to choose from High Quality or Standard Quality Animated videos. Standard quality takes less than 30 seconds to load the animation on watch and High quality takes less than 90 seconds.

- Digital dial text direction: Top, Centre, Bottom

- Over 20 Digital fonts available

- Choose from a list of categories based on the below themes.
. Abstract
. Animation
. Background
. Blur
. Celebrations
. Clouds
. Earth
. Energy
. Flowers
. Lights
. Loop
. Love
. Music
. Nature (Includes Beautiful Natural landscape, Sunrise, Sunset, Scenery, Birds, Cats, Animals)
. Space
. Spiral
. Sports
. Water

So, what are you waiting for? Download this unique app and enjoy a special and amazing experience


Note : Only supports Android Wear Watches.
(Watches like Samsung Gear, DZ09 ,Sony smartwatch are not supported)
