Description

- Watchmaker needed to load the watch face Download here : http://goo.gl/FMxUfY
- This is a APK package contains a 12 and 24 hours version watch face in english and international date setup that will load automatically in Watchmaker after install. For Android Wear 5

- Watch Face designed for optimal readability, beauty and precision with the moto 360 display in mind.

- New and improved stopwatch now with slide up and slide down watch face effect.
stopwatch mode include a new screen in the middle dial with : step counter, digital stopwatch and heart rate monitor.

- Scrolling weather conditions, temperature and humidity.

- Date in watch mode, digital clock in stopwatch mode.

- Different dimmed design for watch and stopwatch.

- Watch and phone power reserve.

- Watch face compatible with all round displays and now square.

- Now with a new watch face in Cyan dimmed mode flavor.
