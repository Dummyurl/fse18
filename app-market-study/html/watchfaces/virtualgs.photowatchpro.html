Description

Your photo is your watch face! Photo Watch Pro is the fastest way to show photo on your Android Wear watch!

NOTE:
• Gear S2/S3 users please download Photo Watch Pro from Galaxy Apps
• This is for Android Wear 1.x devices. Android Wear 2 users please download Photo Watch 2 with support of complications!

Choose from one of 3 watch faces provided, then you can:
• Take a photo from camera
• Select from best picked pictures on Internet
• Select a picture from your own photo album or Google Photos
• Share picture from other apps, including painting apps

The photo will show up as background on the watch face immediately! It is that simple!

You can also
• Customise the text (including emojis) - add a special message for your love one!
• Change font - default, LED, LCD, retro computer, fancy or comic font
• Change text color and size
• Change time display position
• Show translucent photo in ambient mode with 3 level of brightness

3 watch faces provided: photo, analog and digital. All are interactive watch faces that you can tap to show the photo without the time display.
