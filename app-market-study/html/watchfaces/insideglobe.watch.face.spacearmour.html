Description

It is definitely not among the masses. It emulates the gauges in space crafts and the result is stunning new designs. You could feel a kind of space armour on your wrist. You would appreciate the feeling of uniqueness, once you had it on your wrist.


What you get
★ It monitors your heartbeat.
★ It counts your steps.
★ It shows the temperature of your location.
- Set the location settings in your phone.
- Set the time interval to see the temperature or stop it.
★ It has a unique phone unlock counter.
- You could know the number of times you unlocked your phone in a day.
★ Battery indicator shows the battery life of the phone and the watch.
★ Has day and date indicator.

What is unique
★ Stunning new designs
★ Well-crafted, attractive round and square dials.
★ Face colors can be easily changed.

• Motorola 360 (1st , 2nd gen and sport)
• Huawei Watch
