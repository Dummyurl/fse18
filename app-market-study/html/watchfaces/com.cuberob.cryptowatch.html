Description

A clean and minimalistic android wear watch face that provides you with the latest Bit- or altcoin price index! In addition it features a Portfolio and CoinMarketCap Watch app to stay up to date from your wrist.

⌚ - Features
- Watchface with price ticker
- Portfolio watch app & complication support
- Ticker complication support (use cryptowatch with other watchfaces on Android Wear 2.0)
- Coin Market Cap rankings watch app
- AM/PM mode
- Discrete mode (hide currency symbol)
- Customizable token, exchange, currency & colors
- Auto price refresh or force refresh by clicking

📱 - Features
- Portfolio
- Ticker homescreen widgets
- Portfolio homescreen widget
- CoinMarketCap rankings
- Token detail page with historic chart data [in beta] and link to projects
- Watch face configuration screen
- Tasker (external app) support: retrieve prices into Tasker variables
