Description

Get your weather when it really matters™ with The Weather Network app for Android. This free app is accurate and easy-to-use, giving you the forecast at your fingertips with access to the most in-depth weather news and video. Stay ahead of storms with notifications, and watch the weather system with our satellite and weather radar maps.

KEY FEATURES:

• Detailed WEATHER FORECASTS including current observations and temperatures, short term (36 hours), long term (14 days) and hourly forecasts that are updated every 15 minutes to ensure accuracy and reliability

• Severe weather and storm ALERTS to notify you when a storm is heading your way. See a red alert banner on the affected cities and regions and can click-through for more info (Available for Canada & U.S. locations)

• Multiple MAP layers, including weather radar and satellite

• PRECIPITATION START & STOP times within a 10 minute window

• FOLLOW ME functionality to give you the most accurate forecast within 1 km (0.6 mi) of your current location

• WIDGETS in multiple sizes so you can see the weather right from your home screen

• ANDROID WEAR support with The Weather Network watch app that displays current conditions and alerts at a glance and a custom watch face that displays expected precipitation within the next hour

• Discover our community’s PHOTOS & VIDEOS and upload your own to our gallery

• Available in English and French, based on your device settings


TIPS:

• Scroll down to access weather forecasts, news, videos and maps at a glance
• Tap on the blue details button next to the current conditions to access yesterday’s high & low temperature and more details like wind speed and direction, humidity, cloud ceiling, and sunrise & sunset times
• The menu icon on the top left provides access to all the content available on the app including notifications, app settings and FAQs
• Add a location by tapping the city name at the top center of your screen
• Tap on any of the at-a-glance cards in the main overview screen to access more details
• Pull down to refresh for the most up-to-date weather data
