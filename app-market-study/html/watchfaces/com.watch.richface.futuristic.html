Description

Interactive Futuristic Watch Face

★★★ NEW!!! Interactive watch face for changing colors and showing forecasts with just tap on the screen ★★★

★★★ IMPORTANT: Interactive watch faces require the latest version of Android Wear, which is rolling out over the next few weeks. Hang tight for the update! ★★★

★★★ Features: ★★★

✔ ★ Changing palette colors by tap ★
✔ ★ Forecasts for 3 days ★
✔ Weather(current weather)
✔ Time, Date and Month
✔ Google FIT
✔ Stopwatch
✔ Menu with shortcuts
✔ Music player
✔ Battery indicator
✔ Phone battery indicator

★★★ Settings: ★★★
✔ Custom color configuration
✔ Custom hour format
✔ Smooth seconds settings
✔ Screen time settings
✔ Full or simple ambient mode
✔ Weather provider
✔ Weather refresh option
✔ Temp unit
✔ Date format

★★★ Tip ★★★

If the watch face is not shown on your watch after re-sync, try these steps:

1. Disconnect devices (watch and phone)
2. Uninstall the watch face
3. Restart watch and connect device again
4. Then finally install the watch face
