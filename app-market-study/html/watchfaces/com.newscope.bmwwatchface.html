Description

This bundle of watch faces showcases different BMW models. Each resembles a speedometer design of timeless masterpieces from BMW – including the visionary BMW VISION NEXT 100.
BMW created these watch faces for all fans as part of the celebrations marking the brands 100st anniversary in 2016. The centenary wasn’t just an opportunity to highlight the pioneering achievements of the past, but also to look forward to the exciting times that lie ahead of us.

Features:
Most watchfaces display additional data like a pedometer and the battery level. You can switch through these functions by tapping the watch face once. A double tap in the middle of the screen reveals the respective BMW model.

These models are featured:

BMW Vision Vehicle:
A vehicle that recognises your needs, reacts accordingly and also enhances your perception. A car like that empowers you to become the ""Ultimate Driver"" - the best driver you can possibly be.

BMW i8:
The BMW i8 stands out by its progressive design that is epitomized by eye-catching gullwing doors.
Thanks to its plug-in hybrid drivetrain it features the responsiveness of a sports car, while fuel consumption and emission levels are comparable to a compact car.

BMW M1:
The 1978 BMW M1 was the first mid-engined BMW sports car. As the first production model of the newly founded Motorsport GmbH it started the M legacy.

BMW Z8:
This roadster was launched at the turn of the millennium as an homage to the BMW 507 of the 1950s. Built as a state-of-the-art super sports car it offered everything BMW had in store in terms of innovative technology in 1999.

BMW 3.0 CSL:
In 1971 the BMW 3.0 CSL became an instant sensation on the streets – and it also dominated the touring-car scene. It remains one of the most beloved BMW models to this day.

BMW Isetta:
In the BMW Isetta German engineering met Italian design. It caused excitement when it was introduced in March 1955. Small, eggshaped and with bubble-type windows it was unlike anything that had been seen before.
