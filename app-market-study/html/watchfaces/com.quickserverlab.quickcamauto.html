Description

Drive Cam (Formerly Quick Cam Auto) helps you take pictures when driving with just a hand wave.

When we drive we come across many interesting moments and we feel bad that we can't capture it without stopping or our camera is not ready. We feel memories are important so “Drive Cam” will help you capture these moments.

Once you setup your phone on the dashboard just open the app. Now any time you come across any thing interesting you just have to wave your hand on top of the phone (close to proximity sensor) to tell DriveCam to capture the picture.

Key features:
- Voice Notification – when picture is taken and saved
(needs to be enabled from preferences section)
- Monitors proximity sensor for commands
- Wake up screen when picture is taken
- Capture Delay now configureable in preferences
- Show preview button to align phone during setup
- Added support for Geo Tagging (configureable)
- Supports Advanced Controls [#]
- Exposure & Zoom settings changes on UI [#]
- Focusing Mode [#]
- Flash Mode [#]
- Scene Mode [#]
- White Balance [#]
- Changing of location to save picture [#]
- Switching camera (back & front) - selfie support
- Countdown timer
- Tasker plugin [#]
- Android wear supported

[#] Pro Only

Languages:
- English, Japanese, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Thai, Hebrew, Portuguese, Spanish and Korean

Enjoy and have a safe journey!

For any questions, problems or wishes please email us. Thank you for using Drive Cam.
