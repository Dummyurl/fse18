Description

Wear Interval Timer is the one and only interval training timer you need for your Android Wear smartwatch.

Timing workouts using smartphones is a thing of the past. The best interval timer is always with you on your wrist where you can check your progress at a glance.

This is a great timer for CrossFit training, high intensity interval training (HIIT), Tabata, running, cycling, boxing, martial arts and many others.

FEATURES
- vibrates during transition between workout and rest, no need to check your watch
- customizable timer themes to suit your every mood
- customizable workout and rest duration settings
- customizable number of rounds with total time calculation for entire session
- progress indicator for number of rounds
- pause timer during interruptions
- last custom settings are stored
- option to beep your phone every transition

USAGE

On your watch:

From the timer view, swipe to the left to set workout duration
From work duration view, swipe to the left to set rest duration
From rest duration view, swipe to the left to customize the timer's appearance by selecting a custom theme
Swipe to the right to go back to previous views
To dismiss the app, in the timer view, swipe to the right

On your phone:

Select a custom theme and tap on buy to unlock that theme and use it in your watch

COMPATIBLE ANDROID WEAR DEVICES:
Samsung Gear Live, LG G Watch, LG G Watch R, Sony Smartwatch 3, Asus ZenWatch and/or Moto 360 running Android 4.3 or higher
