Description

The Earth 3D watch face displays a 3D rotating earth with separate cloud layer and the time displayed.

The watch face will automatically install to your android wear device after installing the companion app on your handheld device. It may take a couple minutes for it to appear on the watch due to the number of high resolution images. Set the Earth 3D watch face on your watch after installation is complete.

For more applications visit our website at http://www.RockingPocketGames.com
