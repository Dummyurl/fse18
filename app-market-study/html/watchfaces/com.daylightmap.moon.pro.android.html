Description

Spectacular, interactive 3D simulation of the moon, right down to the shadows in the craters and earthshine on the dark side. Touch screen control changes date and time as seen from your location - the phase tracks your every move. Or use two fingers to spin the moon itself: see the far side!

One of Yahoo! News' Best Stargazing Apps, and one of Appolicious' Best Moon Phase Android Apps

Features:
▶ For any date/location, instantly shows phase, crescent angle, rise/set times, and nearest syzygies
▶ New in version 7: shows supermoons, blue moons, and black moons
▶ Displays lunar eclipses (beta)
▶ Change phase by touchscreen or direct date entry
▶ 2 finger drag rotates the 3D moon simulation
▶ Lunar Calendar shows month's phases at a glance
▶ Data pane gives additional details: azimuth, altitude, distance, apogee, perigee, zenith, and more
▶ Android Wear watch face shows the same imagery, plus (optionally) abbreviated data
▶ Supplies moon data & imagery via complications to other Wear 2 watch faces
▶ Choice of imagery: High definition or Maximum realism
▶ Share moon image & data via social media
▶ Fully optimized for Chromebooks and tablets
▶ Notifications for major phases, also optimized for Android Wear
▶ Live wallpaper
▶ Resizable home screen widget, with optional moon info text
▶ Dynamic shadows of surface features (craters, mountains, etc.)

For a bit of fun, check out our trailer video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vc42F2Awxi8
