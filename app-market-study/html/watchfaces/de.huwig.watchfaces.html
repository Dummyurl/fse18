Description

Enjoy 27 different watchfaces on your phone or a smartwatch that is capable of running apps, like

* Callisto 100
* Omate TrueSmart
* Simvalley AW-414, AW-420

as well as all Android Wear devices like

* Samsung Gear Live
* LG G Watch

On Android Wear do a long click on the clock to get to the list of the clock faces and select the one you like.

These companion watches are also supported:

* Sony Smartwatch 1
* Sony Smartwatch 2

DOES NOT WORK ON SAMSUNG GALAXY GEAR (but on the Gear Live running Android Wear)!

Swipe horizontally to switch the face and vertically to change the theme (some faces don't have themes). Double-tap for options.

The app starts in demo mode and sometimes displays a text "demo version" over the face. To disable the text, you have to buy the app via an in-app purchase. You can access the in-app purchase by double-tapping the screen.

The app is already licensed on the Omate TrueSmart and no purchase is necessary. For all other watches: if you purchase the app, then in the settings the option

* install as lockscreen
* developer mode

will be enabled.

To develop your own faces, go to

https://github.com/kurthuwig/faces

to get the source of all watch faces and a tutorial how to use them.
