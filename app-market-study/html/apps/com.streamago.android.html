Description

Be Spontaneous
Can't stop laughing about something that happened today? Bored and looking for someone to chat with? Go live and share your voice with anyone you like. Do something unexpected and your friends and strangers will notice you!

Be Yourself
With Streamago, you decide who you want to live chat with. Choose between private and public to select who to invite - a close group of friends or the whole world! Go live and share your livestreams on Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, Messenger and more. Live video chat makes interacting fun and easy. Join livestreams and show some love by commenting and giving candies, hearts and more.

Leaderboard
Become popular and rise to the top of the leaderboard

Don't Miss Out
Follow your favorite people and we’ll notify you when they go live, so you never miss a moment. Interact in real-time or watch replays if you missed any livestreams.

Explore
See what's happening live on Streamago TV. Catch who's on-air in real-time in your social circles and from around the world. Access livestreams and replay broadcasts.

Tips:
- Pinch to zoom in and out
- Double tap to switch from front to back camera, and vice-versa
- Two fingers tap to turn On and Off the light
- To create an HD livestream, choose “high” quality in “Settings”
- Respond to user comments on the video by talking to them in real-time
- Broadcasters can now block or mute viewers
- Tap on chat messages and access user info

Questions or feedback? We’d love to hear from you - support@streamago.com
