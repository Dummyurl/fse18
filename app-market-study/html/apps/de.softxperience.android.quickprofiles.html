Description

A simple profiles app. Create profiles with ringer & volume settings, ringtone, brightness, timeout, also state of wifi, bluetooth & hotspot.
Also create shortcuts to profiles on home screen.

Please note: App is NOT intended to switch profiles automatically! Just manual!

For widget & icons get the Pro add-on!

Changing GPS, 2G/3G is NOT possible due to limitations of Android!
