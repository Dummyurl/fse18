Description

Thirty Two brings true retro flavor to your Android Wear watch. Show off your smart watch in fantastic 32x32 pixel glory. This watch face simulates various classic LED and LCD dot matrix displays.

IMPORTANT: If you see a permission dialog asking for 'Identity' permissions it's not because the watch face actually does anything with it. I'm still investigating and trying to get rid of this. Rest assured however, the watchface isn't doing anything with any of your personal information.
Features:

- Many different color themes
- Configurable anti aliasing
- Configurable ticks
- Configurable width of clock hands
- Superb burn-in protection
- Compatible with all Android Wear watches
