Description

★★★The first smart door lock for Europe now on Google Play★★★

A small upgrade to your door lock, making life so much easier. With Nuki and your smartphone, you can now easily retrofit your door lock for smart access. Nuki Smart Lock is mounted on the inside of your existing door lock and ready to be used within 3 minutes without any further assistance. No screws, no drills.

Here is what Nuki can do for you:
• Turns your smartphone into a smart key
• Auto-Unlock automatically unlocks the door as you approach
• Share access codes with friends & loved-ones easily via SMS/Whatsapp right from the Nuki app
• Lock and unlock your door remotely with Nuki Bridge via Wifi
• Check the status of your lock anytime and anywhere on your smartphone
• Never loose any keys again
• Compatible with Android Wear to control your lock from your smartwatch

Get Nuki Smart Lock now from the Nuki Shop: https://shop.nuki.io/

Your benefits with Nuki:

Smart - Nuki unlocks your door automatically when you come home and locks it again when you leave. The key stays in the lock while your phone stays in your pocket.

Simple - Nuki Smart Lock is mounted on the inside of your existing door lock and ready to be used within 3 minutes without any further assistance. No screws, no drills.

Secure - Stay in control of your Nuki Smart Lock by checking the status on your smartphone anytime and by managing access permissions for your friends and loved-ones.

Remote - The Nuki Bridge brings your Smart Lock online, so you can manage and control it remotely with the Nuki app anytime and anywhere.

Installation requirements for Nuki Smart Lock:
• Bluetooth Smart Ready phone (Bluetooth 4.0 and higher)
• Android 4.4 and higher
• Operating temperature: 10–40 °C
• Designed for indoor use only
• Door with Euro profile cylinder and emergency and danger function

You can check the compatibility of your door lock with Nuki here: www.nuki.io/check.
