Description

To make your mobile banking simpler and easier than ever, ASB’s Mobile Banking app is absolutely loaded with smart features.

It offers you greater choice about how you make payments. You can pay anyone to their email address or mobile number. Or if you prefer the old school bank account number method, you can still do that, too. You can pay for Trade Me won items. You can even pay for online purchases at participating merchants. Pay anyone you like, anywhere you are, any time you want.

Plus there are plenty of other great features. ‘Quick Balance’ lets you view up to three designated account balances without logging in, and you can now access your ‘Quick Balance’ and make a ‘Quick Transfer’ on your Android Wear device. And when you do have to log in the optional 5-digit PIN makes it faster. There’s an ATM & Branch locator plus Transfers, Payments, Foreign Exchange rates, the ability to manage your credit card limit or pay off your home, personal or business loan faster, all from your smartphone. Keep in control of your finances by getting real-time notifications about your account and other banking related activity.

To use the ASB Mobile app, you need to be registered for FastNet Classic (ASB internet banking). Please call 0800 MOB BANK (0800 662 226) to register. It’s free to use the ASB Mobile app, but your usual data costs and standard FastNet Classic transaction and service charges will apply.
