Description

This app is powered by our huge database, the biggest one in Japan.
This app is made by "LIFULL HOME'S-Real Estate sale & rent" which is one of the biggest real estate portal sites in Japan.
You can search for real estate properties both for rent and sale with a smooth interaction.
The app has been updated with a lot of new design and requested useful functions.
You can freely set conditions like train lines, stations, cities.
You can bookmark properties, save search conditions, and browse your search history.
You can check routes from where you are now to destination real estate companies.
