Description

Get Direction Information, Weather Condition & Pic your Picture on Map to help you Remember Awesome Moments in the way they were...

Features
+ Pin a Pic on Map | Google Map as your Personal Photo Album
- Take a Picture & App will automatically Pin it to Location on Google Map (Privately)
- Press on Pinned Mark to See a Pic then
- Press & Hold to see location on Google StreetView
+ Automatic Day/Night: App will set to Dark/Light theme based on time
+ Location & Weather: Get Simple Weather Condition Information
+ Custom Compass Image: Go to Preference & Select an Image for Compass
+ Floating Compass for Android Wear SmartWatch

Note:
* Azimuth: 0 >> North
* Azimuth: 180 >> South
* Best Result for Compass is when device be in flat state: Pitch = 0 & Roll = 0

[Phone]
Tip: Press & Hold on Compass to Pause Sensors. Then you can check carefully XYZ of Sensors.
Tip: Move Floating Compass to Right Side of Display to Minimize it.

[Watch]
Tip: Press & Hold on Floating Compass to see the location on Google Map.
Tip: Click pinned mark on map then Press & Hold on popup window to see the location on Google StreetView.
Tip: Move Floating Compass to Right Side of Display to Minimize it.
