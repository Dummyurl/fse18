Description

Connect to your Genesis G80, G90 or Hyundai Genesis to access your Genesis Connected Services or Blue Link features.

**********

Android version 4.4 and higher required. Supported Devices: Galaxy S6, S6 Edge, S6 Edge+, S5, S4, Note 5, Note 4, Note 3, HTC One, Nexus 6, Nexus 5, LG G4, and G3. Compatible Devices: Note Edge, Galaxy S3, Droid Turbo, Moto X, and Droid Maxx.

**********

An active Genesis Connected Services or Blue Link subscription is required to login and access the Intelligent Assistant App. Use your Genesis.com or MyHyundai.com login email and password to access the app.

**********

An active Remote or Guidance package may be required to access all the Genesis Connected Services or Blue Link features listed. For technical assistance contact: www.hyundaiusa.com/loginhelp.

**********

Intelligent Assistant App Highlights:
• Combines Genesis Connected Services or Hyundai Blue Link features, data from the vehicle, the owner’s smartphone and the internet with multiple layers of intelligence to send reminders and notifications.
• Saves owners time with pre-drive app reminders and notifications

With the Intelligent Assistant App you can:
• Receive calculated departure messages for synced calendar events with location
• Receive proactive notifications to remotely start and cool or heat your vehicle based on current temperature
• Remotely start your vehicle
• Search and send Points of Interest to your vehicle
• Remotely unlock or lock the door
• Check if your doors are locked or unlocked
• Remotely activate the horn and lights
• Find your car
• Access your saved POIs
• Make a service appointment
• Access Genesis Connected Services or Blue Link Customer Care
• Access maintenance information and Monthly Vehicle Report
• Five Genesis widgets for Remote Features, Parking, POI search & Send to Car, and more

Genesis app now supports Android Wear features. Use voice command or Smartwatch menu to access select features.

With the Genesis Smartwatch App you can:
• Remotely start your vehicle
• Remotely unlock or lock the door
• Remotely activate the horn and lights
• Car Finder
• Parking Meter


For technical assistance contact: www.hyundaiusa.com/loginhelp
As a security measure to protect your account, this app is not supported on rooted devices.
