Description

Rubik's Cube for Android Wear was originally designed for use with an Android Wear device, however it can also be used on any Android phone or tablet! :)

Drag the center to rotate the cube.
Drag the side to turn the layers of the cube.
Pinch the background to change the zoom on the cube.

If you have an Android Wear device, see how fast you can solve a cube on your watch!
If you don't have an Android Wear device, see how fast you can solve a cube on a watch on a phone!
