Description

Leading timesheet app for tracking work hours. Use the timer to log task times and get reports based on the time usage. All the time logs are synced to the cloud and you can track time also in the web version, desktop app, Chrome extension and with an Android watch - all your data is safely under one account.

Toggl desktop rarely works behind a proxy. The site works great, but is a website, and cannot offer the smooth and efficient user experience the applications can.
Victor Elias
It's an okay app for its core functionality. Even though I understand and agree with the limitations for visualizing the data in the mobile compared to desktop, I believe the mobile experience should be really focused on quick start/stop timer interactions. The only facilitation I saw was the stop notification, but none for quick starting a timer. A few really simple ideas would be: - A default project just like there is on desktop; - A simple (and please, small) widget that is configured for a specific project and can be toggled from the home screen; - A public action defined for other apps to quickly toggle timers. Especially useful with launchers like Nova for defining custom gestures in home screen to toggl.
