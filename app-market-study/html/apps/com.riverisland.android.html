Description

You can now get your River Island fix on the go with our new Android app. Whether you want to shop on the move, view our trends or check in to your local River Island store, it’s all available at the touch of a button. With new products arriving online daily, you can be the first to shop Just Arrived wherever you are, and share what you've bought with your friends. You can also check stock in your nearest River Island store to avoid disappointment on your next shopping trip.
