Description

Beautiful and fully customizable interactive watch face. The first ever watch face with Wear Mini Launcher NOW support. With both Wear Mini Launcher and Wear Mini Watch Face installed on your Android Wear Device, you can launch your favorite app, or access toggles / widgets / mini apps right on your watch face.

At this very beginning, we have limited selection of toggle, widget and mini app, we will keep working hard to bring more convenience to your fingertip.

Features
Interactive Actions (Android Wear 1.3 and Wear Mini Launcher 4.1.0 or newer required)
- 4 interactive zones available
- Launcher any app
- Toggle phone WiFi
- Find your phone

Customizable Style
- 12 watch face backgrounds
- 3 widget backgrounds
- 5 dial styles
- 2 border styles
- Option to show analog watch hand

Customizable Color
- Watch hand
- Dial
- Digital display background

Compatible Devices

- Motorola Moto 360
- LG G Watch
- Samsung Gear Live
- LG G Watch R
- Sony SmartWatch 3
- Asus Zenwatch
- LG G Watch Urbane (Android Wear version)
