Description

Bold. Minimal. Modern.

A collection of 5 minimal yet bold watch faces for Android Wear. This collection features unique watch faces purposefully designed for glanceability. Developed for both form and function these faces place a strong emphasis on minimalism. Each watch face contains multiple accent colors and features for added personalization.

Watch Face Collection Includes:

NOVA
A bold digital watch face with subtle second hand animation.

PULSE
A sleek watch face featuring time as text, as well as the current date.

SPHERE
An abstract take on time. Designed to be both unique and stylish.

CHRONO
A minimal analog watch face, featuring the current date. Designed for both round and square Android Wear watches.

ULTRA
A minimal analog watch face with fun customization options. Complications include watch battery %, current date and second digital time. Designed for both round and square Android Wear watches.


Features:
- 5 unique watch faces for one price
- Minimal design for added glanceability
- 16 unique color choices for added personalization
- Complications include watch battery %, current date, digital time
- Subtle yet smooth animation
- All faces display well on both round and square watches
- Chrono and Ultra feature custom round and square versions
