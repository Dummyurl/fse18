Description

Winter Sports is the official app of Professional Ski Instructors of America and American Association of Snowboard Instructors (PSIA-AASI). Beautiful GPS tracking, ski run analytics, snow reports for thousands of resorts, zoomable resort maps, activity tracking, customizable goals, a social network, leaderboards and ski lessons by snow pros. The app includes many innovations, including voice guidance.

FEATURES

TRACK YOUR SKIING, SNOWBOARDING AND OTHER SPORTS
Simply switch tracking on at the beginning of your ski day, and put the phone in your pocket. The app will automatically track your runs, speed, vertical, goals and achievements, and present it in beautiful charts. You can share your stats with your friends.

SNOW REPORTS AND RESORT MAPS
Click on snow reports and search thousands of resorts for the latest snow and powder information, number of open trails, lifts and mountain statistics. Resorts include zoomable maps.

FRIENDS AND SOCIAL
Connect with your skiing buddies through our social network.

SET TRAINING GOALS
You can become the King or Queen of the mountain! Set goals, like vertical, or the number of runs to keep you motivated and compete with other members of our network.

BECOME A LEADER
Watch stats and connect with the leaders with global leaderboards.

VOICE
Provides audio cues, impress your friends on the lift!

TRAINING PLANS
Improve your skiing, snowboarding with exclusive training programs

INTERVAL TRAINING
Improve your endurance with interval training

STATISTICS
View beautiful statistics and analytics.

WEARABLES
Supports Android Wear. Includes a beautiful watchface and a wearable app. No need to pull your phone from your pocket, get notifications directly on you watch.
