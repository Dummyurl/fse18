Description

Ever needed to quickly lock your phone when it's not in your hand?

Wear Phone Lock is the answer!

Now you can take back control of your phone, even when it's not nearby! Use Wear Phone Lock to remotely lock your device, as if you've just pressed the power button.

Simply tap the lock button from within the watch app, and your phone will lock straight away.

IMPORTANT:
Smart Lock will be disabled temporarily when you lock your screen with Wear Phone Lock. This means that you won't be able to unlock your device with your fingerprint or face, if it was locked from your watch (you will always be able to unlock with your backup PIN, pattern or passcode). This is a security feature of the Android system and cannot be changed.

This app uses the Device Administrator permission.
•This setting must be enabled before Wear Phone Lock can lock your device.
•This is necessary as it grants Wear Phone Lock the 'Lock your screen' permission, and is used for the sole purpose of locking the phone's screen when the lock button is pressed on your watch.
•This change is easily reversible and can be disabled at any time from within Wear Phone Lock's settings page.

Please note: To uninstall Wear Phone Lock, you must first disable its administrator permissions, by going to the 'Settings' page in the app. You will then be able to uninstall the app normally.
