Description

Use LevelUp to save 15 minutes every day at breakfast, lunch, dinner or your coffee run. Browse your favorite local spots, order ahead and pay easily all within the app.

• Open LevelUp to see all the places near you.
• Browse through the menu and when you’re ready tap “Place My Order”.
• Most orders are ready in 7-15 minutes. We’ll let you know when it's going to be ready and where to grab it in-store.
• When you arrive, no need to wait in line! Your food is paid for and ready for you. Just grab your food from the pickup area and enjoy your meal.

LevelUp gives you the power to order ahead your coffee, lunch, afternoon snack, breakfast, second breakfast and dinner with a single tap and have it ready when you get there.

What type of food do you like? We’ve got all the favorites like pizza, donuts, burritos, sushi and burgers. Looking for a specific type of cuisine? We have everything from Korean to Spanish to Polish. Maybe you’re thirsty? Search for coffee, smoothies or a healthy juice! Whether you’re looking for some late night eats, low-carb ice cream or a good ramen restaurant we’ve got you covered!

We’re adding more restaurants daily, so let us know if your favorite restaurant or cafe isn’t available and we’ll get them on board!
