Description

Now, take your bank with you wherever you go with the ADCB Watch App on your Android Watch. Track your bank accounts and credit card transactions absolutely anywhere and at any time straight from your wrist.
Things you can do with the ADCB Watch App:
• Check Bank Account Balances and Credit Card Limits
• Track the last 5 transactions

To start the ADCB Watch App experience,
• Download the ADCB Watch App on your Android Phone
• Carry out the one-time activation process from your Android Phone
• And your Android Watch App is ready for use
