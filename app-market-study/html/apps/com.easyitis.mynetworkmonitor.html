Description

Ever gone out of coverage and didn't realize?

This small and simple utility will ensure that this doesn't happen again. It monitors your network connection (upon request) and notifies you as soon as you lose network connection.

NEW!! Android Wear support - now get notified on you Android Watch when your phone goes out of coverage. Handy!

NEWER!! Get reminded at reboot to start network monitoring.

NEWEST!! Choose to be alerted when network is restored.
