Description

See your timeline and your lists and update your status directly from your Android Wear watch.

Features:
* See your last 10 tweets from your timeline or your lists
* Save links in tweets to read them later
* Retweet, favorite them
* Reply to them by voice
* Post a new tweet
* Show embedded images of tweets

To start posting a new tweet, say 'Ok, Google' then 'Start New Tweet' and say the text for your tweet when prompted. You can also find the 'New Tweet' icon in your application list so you can start it from there.

Big thanks to Diego De Barbieri and Евгений Дрямин for the translations!

Expect more features to come. Bug reports and suggestions are welcome!
Also, if you can help me with translations, please send me an email. Thanks!
