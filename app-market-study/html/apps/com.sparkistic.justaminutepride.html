Description

Just A Minute™ is a minute-forward watch face design for Android Wear. This special Pride Edition lets you show your pride in being who you are and for support of diversity and inclusion.

UPDATED for Android Wear 2 and iOS iPhone users! Simply long-press the watch face to change options. On devices supporting Android Wear 2, the companion mobile app is no longer required. Now supports complications.

* We will be donating 15% of all revenue from purchases of Just a Minute Pride Edition to The Trevor Project, which is the leading national organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and questioning (LGBTQ) young people. For more information see thetrevorproject.org

Stuck in meetings all day and wonder how many minutes are left? Waiting for the bus you know comes in a few minutes? Just glance at your watch to see the minutes past the hour!

Just A Minute - make every minute count (with PRIDE)!

Note: this is a special LGBT Pride edition of the standard Just A Minute™ watch face: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sparkistic.justaminute
