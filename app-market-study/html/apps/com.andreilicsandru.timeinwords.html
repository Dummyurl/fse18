Description

Time in Words displays the time in an elegant fashion, lighting up the corresponding words!
Available in English, German and Swedish.
Carefully designed for all Round and Square smartwatches running Android Wear!

I am aware of the watchface missing for users running Android 5.x and I'm working to release a fix asap! Thank you for the bug reports and for your patience!

💡Permissions
Time in Words requires permission to read the CALENDAR. It will ask two times to enable access to Calendar. You only need to allow access if you use the CALENDAR.
The watchface may request the permission to read calendar events plus confidential information, that is how the Calendar permission is described as on Android Marshmallow. If you are unsure about this, you can safely refuse the access, it will not affect other functionalities.

v2.1 MAJOR OVERHAUL
Time in Words now features a unique set of five screens that can be easily switched between by gently tapping the screen on its edges!
Manage your appointments in the CALENDAR screen, where you can double tap to open the Wear Calendar!
Check the WEATHER before going outside unprepared! (Weather provider is OpenWeatherMap)
See the current DATE and BATTERY LEVEL beautifully shaped by the grid of letters!
Check the screenshots above for more details!

⌚ Features
★ Weather, calendar, date and battery level
★ Specify Weather update interval
★ Notification cards awareness
★ Custom background and letters color
★ Change Ambient letters color to match Interactive letters color
★ Enable/disable IT IS in Ambient mode
★ Enable/disable inactive letters
★ Enable/disable AM/PM indicator

Time in Words Watch Face is perfectly compatible with Round and Square Smartwatches!

Time in Words is a premium watchface and thoroughly tested.
In case you encounter any problems or if you have any questions or suggestions, send me an e-mail at andrei.licsandru.dev@gmail.com. I will be more than happy to help you!

Time in Words requires Android Wear 5.0+ and Android 4.3+ (Jelly Bean).

If the watch face does not appear within a few minutes from the install, run 'Resync apps' from 'Settings' in Android Wear.
If the watch face still does not sync:
- Uninstall the app
- Disconnect the smartwatch from the phone
- Restart the smartwatch
- Reinstall the app

Enjoy!
