Description

The 4Nights Watch Face comes with 10 handcrafted minimal and lovely background images. You can choose between all material design colors and hide or show content you like. This Watch Face just looks awesome everywhere and is a real eyecatcher!


Benefits:
★ Select 1 of 10 handcrafted background images
★ Choose between all material design colors
★ Addons like Show Status icons
★ Background transparency setting
★ Low Battery warning (Status icon)
★ Different font types
★ Energy Safe Mode
★ For round and square Smartwatches
★ And so much more!


As you can see this Watch Face comes with an clear but pretty high customizable settings app!
But the most important point is, this app didn't got build just to look awesome, it is also very battery friendly!


Still something missing or not working? No problem. Just feel free to write us an email or join the community (Link below)
