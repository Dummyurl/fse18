Description

MobileDay® is the fastest, most reliable way to dial into any conference call or online meeting from your smartphone.

Works with any type of phone, any calendar, any conference call bridge—and if you don't have one you can use a MobileDay Bridge for free.

This is the most reliable dialing app out there—a real person tests and validates each dial-in sequence, ensuring that you have dependable access to every call made with MobileDay.

> How One-Touch Dialing Works:

1. MobileDay syncs with your Android calendar.
2. MobileDay detects your conference calls and online meetings and allows you to dial in with one simple tap—no need to remember call-in numbers, PINs, or passcodes.

> Pricing:

MobileDay is a free app.

If you want some special additional features, you can purchase MobileDay Business which gives you the ability to Push a call to any phone and use WiFi.

> Features:

• Auto-detect scheduled events in your calendar.
- Free conference bridge included, or use your own.
• Receive notifications of upcoming meetings.
• Get dependable one-tap access to any conference call or online meeting.
• Rely on MobileDay to get you into every call accurately using human validated dial-in sequences.
• Rejoin dropped calls with one tap.
• Notify attendees when you are running late via text or email with a pre-populated, customizable message.
• Use quick call options to dial anyone in your contacts, or call using the MobileDay keypad.
• Schedule meetings from within the app with your conference dial-in information pre-populated.
• Join conference calls and online meetings on any platform, including WebEx®, GoToMeeting®, Google+ Hangouts, Lync, and more.

> Why use MobileDay?

• The app’s simple, uncluttered, and easy-to-use interface.
• MobileDay complies with the strictest mobile app security standards, so private data such as your location, calendar, and contacts never leaves your device.
• A real person tests and validates each dial-in sequence so you can rely on MobileDay to successfully get you into every call, every time.
