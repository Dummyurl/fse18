Description

Atlas Wristband is the only wearable that automatically recognizes the exercise you’re doing then logs your rep count and analyzes your form.

Use this app to view your workout journal, dive deeper into your data, and get feedback on calories burned, heart-rate zones, form score and more. Atlas Wristband uses 360 degree sensors and the most advanced exercise database and machine-learning.

WORKOUT JOURNAL. Whether you’re interested in cross-fitness, Body-building, weight loss, gym activities or anything in between, Atlas Wristband is perfect for those looking to automatically record workouts and track progress toward fitness goals.

STRENGTH IN NUMBERS. Sync your workout journal effortlessly from the Atlas Wristband with this Atlas Wearables app. In this app you can dive deeper into advanced fitness analytics when you need it most. View muscle focus, heart rate data, calories burned, form evaluation and tunable metrics such as power, stability and velocity. There’s no magic bullet for health and fitness, and you deserve feedback and guidance on what matters to you.

GUIDED WORKOUTS. Sync guided workout programs from the app to your Atlas Wristband to navigate your workouts like a pro. Atlas Coach gives you turn-by-turn fitness helping you hit every muscle group and optimize your workout efficiency.

SYNC WIRELESSLY: Atlas Wristband syncs your hard-earned stats with your iPhone 5 and later to give you complete access to your progress without plugging in.
