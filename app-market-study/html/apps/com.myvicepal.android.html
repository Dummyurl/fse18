Description

MyVicePal makes it easy to track your drinking, BAC and lose weight. Simply add each drink you have with our easy and beautiful app and we'll show you your estimate blood alcohol content (BAC) level and how many calories you're consuming. You'll get a notifications to watch your BAC throughout the evening so you can maintain your best BAC level. Then you can see view your drinking habits over time via our daily, weekly and monthly reports. The more you use the app, the better you'll understand your habits. You can even share your BAC level with your friends too.

MyVicePal was created because we wanted to be able to see how much we were drinking over time and to see if, through technology, we could control our drinking habits in a positive way (less binging, more fun, less regrets).
