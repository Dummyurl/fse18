Description

Important!
Help us bring Hubsic into different languages​​. If you are interested, send an email to contact@newarplabs.com with subject "translations".

Hubsic is a music player for Android 4.0 or higher.

Play the music stored in your device with the best sound quality thanks to the dynamic equalizer whit bass boost and surround sound enhancement.

Search the music in your device and play the results with only one button.

Create your own playlists and edit them easily using two simple gestures: swipe to delete and long press to rearrange the songs. You can save and load them at any time. Created playlists are compatible with other apps like Google Play Music.

Set the preferred volume for each song and forget to adjust the volume every time.

Hubsic is compatible with Android Wear! You can start, stop, change to next song or change the volume from your Smartwatch!

App content:
- Music player for your Android device.
- Dynamic Equalizer with BassBoost and Virtualizer (surround sound enhancement).
- Song search.
- Music library with folder navigation.
- Customizable playlist.
- Volume Control
- Settings
