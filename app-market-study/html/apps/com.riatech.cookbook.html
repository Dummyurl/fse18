Description

Cookbook recipes is one of the best recipes and shopping list apps in the world. We provide recipes for free in more than 20 languages around the world. We designed the Cookbook recipes app to support offline usage.

Cookbook's recipes saver allows you to edit a recipe from the web. The recipe can be modified to your taste and then saved into your recipes collection. Our recipes maganer allows adding new recipes, removing and editing saved recipes.

Are you preparing to start a diet plan? Cookbook's healthy recipes section is here to help. Get cooking ideas for low carb recipes, low calorie recipes, diet recipes to lose weight fast and more for free. Our shopping list and intelligent meal planner allows you to shop and eat healthy.

At Cookbook, we understand you passion for recipes. That is why we build a recipe finder by ingredients. The recipe finder by ingredients allows you to discover easy recipes with ingredients on hand. These yummy recipes come with a build in recipe converter to allow you to change recipe servings. Ingredient quantities are automatically changed based on your serving size.

A wide range of recipes are available in Cookbook like soup recipes, veg recipes, egg recipes, easy recipes, juice recipe, recipes for kids etc.​ Our recipe app in english, hindi, spanish, portuguese etc has offline support. You no longer need any of the old printed recipe books. Upgrade from the old recipe books to Cookbook recipes - a modern, smart and tasty recipes app.

🔍 Search by ingredient
Search for recipes with ingredients you have in your refrigerator. We can convert ingredients into recipes.

👩‍🍳Cooking assistant
Cookbook assistant lets you create intelligent grocery list and shopping list. Cookbook has voice search for recipes built in. Search for recipes based on cuisine, cook time & techniques (grill, bake or fry). Get curated collection of the finest recipes for every event, that too for free. It can also suggest recipes for birthdays, anniversaries, weekend parties and getaways.

Cookbook Recipe manager
Our recipe manager helps you to create meals plans for special occasions. You get reminders for your meal plans and shopping alerts from recipe manager. Shopping for quick and easy recipes is also now simple.

🌎 Popular recipes - Worldwide
Cookbook's trending section has Victoria sponge recipes, tuna casserole recipes, sea bass recipes, bok choy recipes, minestrone recipes, chicken wing recipes, quesadilla recipes, egg salads and mussels recipes.
Our baking section has recipes for cakes, cupcakes, muffins, cheesecake, mug cake recipes, banana cake recipes etc you can cook without a big oven. Recipes for pizza, lasagne, baked salmon etc that need a big oven are also available in Cookbook.

❤︎ Healthy recipes for healthy living
Cookbook has a healthy meal planner with grocery list. Using the meal planner, you can add healthy recipes to the calendar. Ingredients required for the meal plan gets added to your shopping list.

Special Diets
Cookbook helps people suffering from food allergies to dairy, seafood and peanuts eat healthy meals. Cookbook covers people following vegan, paleo, vegetarian and gluten free diets. Keep track of nutrition requirements for fat, calories, carbs and cholesterol using Cookbook. Non-vegetarian recipes for prime ribs, beef stew, pork chops are available in Cookbook. It also covers crock pot and seafood like shrimps and prawns. Appetizers and desserts for Christmas cookies, banana bread, cheesecakes and snickerdoodle are also available.

🚫Offline recipes
Cookbook allows you to download recipes for offline use. You'll never have to worry about internet while cooking your favourite Italian, Spanish or French recipe.
