Description

Quantus Tasks is GTD app that syncs OmniFocus to your Android device. (Requires an OmniFocus account)


# Get Things Done : Add actions to your inbox or immediately assign a project and context.

# Never forget a task : The forecast view shows you at a glance which actions are due soon. Separate project and context views are also available.

# Background syncing : Quantus Tasks works together with OmniFocus on your Mac, iPhone or iPad. Changes you make inside Quantus Tasks become visible inside OmniFocus and vice versa.

# Notifications: Receive notifications when your tasks are due (or x minutes before they become due)


We're working hard on adding new features every few weeks. The goal is to become feature-equivalent to the official iOS OmniFocus app. If you buy the app now you are making a very useful contribution to the app by providing us with the necessary funds to continue development.


## Features we are working on in the coming weeks ##

- Better Project/Context creation
- Better notes/attachment support
- Google Now/Android Wear integration
- Location reminders
- More fine-grained date picking for due/defer dates
- Quick entry widget
