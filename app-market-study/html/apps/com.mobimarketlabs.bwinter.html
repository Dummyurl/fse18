Description

Interactive watch face with:

- date, week, month indication;
- steps counter + additional Steps"B" counter;
- battery levels (watch + phone);
- AM/PM dial;
- Moon phase dial;
- Weather widget (day + 4 days forecast);
- "Night mode";
- custom Ambient mode;
- missed Calls&SMS counters;
- screen customization;
- hands and marks customization;
- TIME2/STEPS2 widget;
- Phone state indicators (Vibro + WiFi&Mobile data on/off);
- Watch face Menu with:
- Stopwatch;
- Tachymeter
- phone apps launcher (up to 5);

... and more.

Working fine on all newest and oldest models: TAG Heuer Connected, moto360 1/2 gen., Sony SW3, LG G W100, R W110, Asus Zen Watch and others.

Configuration supported languages
- English
- Français
- Deutsch
- Українська
- Русский
- 中文 （繁體）

Note: to work correctly reading the number of steps necessary to synchronize the clock with Google Fit app on your smartphone.
