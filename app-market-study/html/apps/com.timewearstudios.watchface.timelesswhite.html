Description

***** PLEASE RATE AND REVIEW******

Timeless is a Watch Face collection for Android Wear
It uses the Official Android Wear API and does not require a 3rd party app to function.
Just install and enjoy!!!! It will Automatically show up on your wear device!

Works for round and square watches
Designed to look timeless like a high quality time piece. These watch faces will never go out of style.
Available in 9 separate colors via separate apps. More coming soon.
Don’t see your color? Email me requests with Hex Code.
Settings app with more options is here!!!!

You can find the settings app inside the android wear app (settings directly on the watch coming soon)
You can choose from a dark or light version of the three watch faces
Tic or sweep of the second’s hand
Hide or show battery percentage and date
Light or dark version of each face

Contact me at timewearstudios@gmail.com with any issues. I will gladly help. Its impossible to provide support through the play store rating/comment system. Thank you
