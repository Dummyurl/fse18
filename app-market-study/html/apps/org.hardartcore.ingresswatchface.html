Description

Unofficial watch face to all Ingress fans and players for your wearables which are running Android Wear.

★ 4 different styles included: Ingress default, Enlightened, Resistance and one bonus!
★ Set your desired background: radial gradient, image or solid color!
★ Set your desired text in your watch face!
★ Set your custom color to any text!
★ Add current date time or digital clock!
★ Custom datetime formats!
★ Hide or show different parts of your watch face!
★ 12 or 24 hour watch display!
★ Battery saver!As an Ingress players, we care a lot about battery life! That's why you can turn off seconds update for even more battery life!
★ This watch face supports short and variable wear notification cards.
★ Customize wear notifications background to solid or transparent.
★ Select which logo you want to put on top of your watch face: Ingress, Enlightened or Resistance!
★ Different hour indicators for your taste!

- And the best part ... a lot more stuff are yet to come in the next few weeks, so if you have any suggestions please contact to us!

All settings are accessible via Android Wear Application on your smartphone, select Ingress WatchFace and click a "gear icon" on it.

This Watch Face works with square and round watches.
The configuration is done entirely via the Android Wear companion app.
In case of any problems please contact us on: damcrew032@gmail.com
Give us a little time to solve your problem and enjoy your new watch face!

If this Application does not appear on your watch try these steps:
- from Android Wear companion app select option 'Resync apps'.
- Uninstall the face
- Disconnect watch from your smartphone
- Connect watch and smartphone again
- Install Ingress WatchFace from Google Play store again.
Google+ Page: https://plus.google.com/u/0/109082237342256928922/about

IMPORTANT! This watch face support only devices which are running with Android Wear 5.0+!

Good luck agents!
