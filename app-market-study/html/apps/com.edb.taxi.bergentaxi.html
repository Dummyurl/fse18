Description

With this app you can book a taxi with one tap, calculate the price in advance and order right from your smartwatch.

The new 07000 app makes taxi travel easier, faster, and smarter. Here are some of the main features:

Taxi booking:
- Automatic positioning: order taxi to your location with just one touch
- Choose to pay with credit cards in the app
- Manual address: enter/search addresses by text
- Favorites: save your favorite addresses and order to any of them
- Book a taxi now or pre-order for later
- Enter destination address and calculate the price in advance
- Order from your Android Wear watch without even picking up your phone

Underway:
- Cancel your taxi from the app (until a car is assigned)
- Get estimated pick-up time as soon as a taxi is assigned
- Follow your taxi on the map to know exactly when you need to be ready

After your journey:
- Register payment cards in the app and get automatic email receipts for all trips - even those taken from taxi stands
- Keep track of previous bookings and order receipts copies right from the app

The 07000 Taxi app covers Bergen, Kristiansand and surrounding areas, including Os, Stord and Nordhordland. It can also be used other places in Norway, including Trondheim, Asker / Bærum, Drammen and Østfold, as well as between Oslo and Gardermoen Airport.
