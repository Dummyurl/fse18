Description

NBK’s mobile banking application allows you to manage your account through your device. The application is available in Arabic and English

It is a safe, convenient, and practical way to undertake all your banking needs with a simply user-friendly interface.

Some of the benefits of the app include:
• Real-Time balance and transactions updates made on your account
• The ability to view the full history of transactions made on your accounts and credit cards
• Transfer funds between your accounts or other NBK customer’s accounts
• Transfer funds locally or internationally
• Ability to transfer money from your credit card to your account (Cash advance)
• Make instant payments to your credit cards
• Pay your mobile and telephone bills with a touch of a button
• Request printed account statements and checkbooks
• Adding Cash Deposit Machine (CDM) and promotion locators
• View participating outlets
• Display FAQs
• Locate your nearest NBK branch or ATM in Kuwait
• Contact NBK through Call Center and Social Media Channels
• Locate branches and ATMs through the Augmented Reality camera in the app
• Travel tips
• Al Jawhara, Loan, and Term Deposit calculators
• View the exchange rate
• Create pre-paid cards with different currencies: in US Dollars, Great Britain Pound, EURO, UAE Dirhams, Egyptian Pound, Indian Rupees, Jordanian Dinar, and Saudi Riyal
And much more

The application is simple, safe, and easy to use.

Download the application today and manage your finance at your own convenience.

For any support issues, please contact NBK Call Center on 1801801 or via What’s App 52221333 and ask for help! Our trained agents will be more than happy to assist you anytime around the clock.
