Description

The Androids don't like lollipops. Get past them to score higher than your friends.

This game is based on Google's Android 5.0 (Lollipop) easter egg.

Thanks to icons8 for the Android L Icon Pack!

Features:
* Use your google account to compare your score with other Androids all around the world!
* Various difficulties. For more experienced and new Android players.
* Choose a path: you can change your Android's direction, you can even go upside down!
* You can choose from multiple backgrounds
* Choose a Droid: Play with Andy, Brandy, Candy, Dandy, Handy, Kandy or Randy!
* Change Andy's color

Share the game with your friends to help all the Androids get to Lollipop Land!
