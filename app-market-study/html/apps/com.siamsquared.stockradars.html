Description

Lost when buying stock? Want to start trading?
With StockRadars it’s never been simpler.
With the majority of stock applications on the market today lacking in both design and practicality, we have listened to investors and created a superior alternative for stock analysis and trading.
Check out our features:
• Radars
Our radars provide intelligent categories that sort significant signal stocks for you daily and with our new Radars Builder, you can now customize your own!
• Trade
Trading in-app is made possible by our unique brokerage partner, providing a fully functional, on-the-go platform.
• Alerts
Alerts keeps track of things for you, signaling when open positions reach your predetermined stock criteria.
• Top Shareholder
The Top Shareholder tab is home to Thailand’s élite investors, providing tailored profiles with an array of data and even the ability to follow your favorites!
• Social Radars
Social Radars provides a continuous in-depth news feed, covering key events and trends.
• Radars School
Radars School is a traders’ playground, offering a large variety of video tutorials on notable app features.
Free Features: Radars (28 Signals Total), Favorite, Portfolio, Radar School, Candlestick Price Graph with Indicators and Drawing.
Premium Features: Radars (200 Signals Total including Fundamental, Technical, Performance, Stochastic, Real Time and Candle selections), Radar Builder, Bid/Offer, Ticker, Portfolio Clinic, Top Shareholder, Alerts and many more!
Download StockRadars for free today, and let us help you become a successful investor!
** Currently available for Securities Exchange of Thailand (SET), Market of Alternative Investment (MAI) and 11 countries worldwide
