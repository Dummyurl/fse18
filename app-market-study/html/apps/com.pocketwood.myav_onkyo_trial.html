Description

MyAV is an unofficial Onkyo AV Receiver Wi-Fi & IR remote control app. Onkyo is a trademark of the Onkyo corporation. This is a free 7 day trial version of the main "Pro" app.
Includes the ability to rename, remove and re-order inputs.
MyAV has a DLNA browser as well as an internet radio, USB & Home Media browser
This app can be viewed portrait or landscape (default) and is designed with high resolution graphics for tablets.

Additionally, MyAV can also control a range of other products (whilst still controlling the volume of your Onkyo). Other devices include Blu-ray players, TVs, set-top boxes, cable & sat.

MyAV now supports the control of older non-networked Onkyo AV Receivers using IR (if you have a device that supports IR like a Huawei Honor7, HTC M8/M9 or Samsung S4/S5/S6 Note 3/4 Tab S etc). Alternatively, you can use the Orvibo AllOne, Keene Kira or Global Cache Ethernet to IR blaster.
A full compatibility list can be found on our site:
http://myav.co.uk/compatiblity.htm
