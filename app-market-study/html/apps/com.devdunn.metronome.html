Description

The Material Metronome is a simple, yet effective app to get a tempo or beat going.


Features

-Start at any tempo from 1 to 250

-Choose between different sounds; Cowbell, Woodblock, Electronic 1, Electronic 2

-Use 11 different bar counts 2/4 to 12/4

-Accent the first note of a bar

-Keep it going while in a different app

-Save configuration to up to four different profiles



Permissions
We do not like permission hungry apps and neither should you but here are the permissions we do request.


• Wakelock - Lets the app keep the screen on if set in the settings

• Vibrator - Relays the tempo on Android Wear devices

• Billing - Adds the ability to donate.
