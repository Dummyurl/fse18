Description

Listed as Top 30 Best Android apps of 2017 by TheNextWeb

We love to ensure that you have the best listening experience. Enjoy a redesigned interface, sync playback position across multiple devices, add notes to episodes, create custom playlists and more!

★★★★★ FEATURES ★★★★★

📚 A catalog of over 300,000 podcasts - Discover many podcasts on different topics, ranging from daily news to entertainment, and broaden your knowledge in any field.

📱💻 Multi-device sync - Share your subscriptions and listen to your favorite podcasts on all your devices, with a single account. So, if you want to go out and your device is charging, you may use another device instead.

🚶 Offline listening - Listen to preselected podcasts offline, perfect for commute or climbing mountains. No signal, no Internet, no problem!

💾 Store downloads to an SD card - No more worries about running out of space and if you can't download podcasts, by setting the download location to keep your device's memory free.

📦 Custom categories - You don't need to scroll down to find particular series. Create custom categories and organize your podcasts the way you like so that you can find them easily.

🎧 Advanced audio player - No more struggles to understand what people say in the podcast. Use speed control, intelligent silence skip and volume boost to ensure that you will have the best listening experience!

⏲️ Sleep timer - You don't have to worry about falling asleep and that the app will be running all night. Once enabled, the sleep timer will stop the app at the time you choose.

★★★★★ PREMIUM FEATURES ★★★★★

Player FM Gold — Level up your daily podcast experience with these cutting-edge features:

😍 Listen on several devices - Easily sync playlists, playback position & resume playback on any device, including our desktop web app!

😎 Custom playlists - Make as many playlists on the app as you want & sync them across devices and the web!

😇 Bookmarks - Save noteworthy quotes from episodes you listened to, note them down and play back effortlessly.

😚 Custom themes - Experiment with colors and create your own themes, to match your style or mood!

😊 Play your collection of media files - Enjoy listening to your local media files, using the Player FM app!

😉 Space saver - Save up to 70% of podcast storage and download as many as you want!

$0.99/month

Player FM Pro — Podcast paradise. All the Gold features, plus the following:

😃 Back up - Save your favorite episodes, even when they're deleted by the publisher, as they're now auto-backed up.

😋 Turbo Fetch - Follow as many shows as you want and get updates early & often, as our cloud servers will closely monitor them.

😎 Personalized search - Look for shows easily, by matching shownotes within your subscriptions and playlists with the personalized search feature.

$3.99/month

Prices above are at annual billing rates. Local currencies may vary and local tax added in some regions.
