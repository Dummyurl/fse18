Description

Every new day: "Mister Schmidt gets up again." On the way to the office, intently focussing at his desk or comfortably watching television with his Renate on the sofa. This watch face shows everyday routine, which is sometimes surprising, of Mister Schmidt's life.

All illustrations were first created in loving detail out of old paper by artist Ljuba Stille and then digitalised.

The Mister Schmidt Watch Face Lite includes:
- a watch face for smart watches (Android Wear)
- a homescreen widget for smart phones
