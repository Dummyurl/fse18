Description

Asparagus is your new personal recipe collection manager. Your digital cookbook. Beautiful modern design, simple interface according to the latest Android design guidelines (Material Design). App features seamless between all of your devices and automatic backup to the cloud. If you own Android Wear or Pebble smartwatch, you can even cook with Asparagus on your wrist.

Start cooking with Asparagus today!

Note: To clip a recipe from website, just share the website in your favorite browser using Asparagus.

Features:
Automatic cloud backup
Seamless multi-device syncing
Recipe clipping (importing) from many popular recipe websites
Fast recipe sharing (app to app)
Customizable recipe folders
Modern colorful, yet simple interface (Material design)
Support for native Android 5.0 Lollipop features
Smart ingredients formatting
Unique servings calculator
Android Wear and Pebble Smartwatch support - Cook with Asparagus on your wrist!
Favorite recipes
Set timer function

Aesthetically Brilliant! Needs a Chrome extension and app! The beautiful Material Design aesthetic is a wondrous delight! The functionality of adding recipes via the Android Chrome browser is simple, intuitive, and flawless. There could be an easier way to delete recipes without having to open the recipe and then remove it, but that is only a minor inconvenience. The only drawback is that you must use an Android Chrome browser to add recipes as there is no extension in the Chrome webstore for use on a computer. A chrome extension would greatly increase the appeal of this app and a broader audience would be able to share/save their recipes to the app. A Chrome app would also be a large boost as the recipes could then be accessed online from any chrome browser!
