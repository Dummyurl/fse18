Description

"Yahoo! News app for Android" offers you the latest news in Japan and around the world.

We cover news on business, entertainment, sports, technology, and more. You will easily find all the news you want, when you want them.

What we do
- Our editing team will bring you the latest news 24/7.
- We provide easy and fun to read articles from the entertainment section.
- You will also find the latest weather forecast, the stock market, and the operation status of trains.
- We will alert you real-time so you won't miss out on the latest news.
- By setting your current location, Yahoo! News will deliver the latest news within the area.

Main Features
- We will deliver you news on hot topics in the morning, day, and night.
- Swipe to change the news category, scroll down to see more news with a different UI.
- We will alert you real-time on breaking news using the push feature.
- You can always check out the latest news on your smartphone home screen by setting the widget for Yahoo! News.
- Share news with friends through mail, social networks, Facebook, Twitter, LINE or any other app you have installed.
