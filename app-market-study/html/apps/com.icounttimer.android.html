Description

Workout timer & counter which is simple, practical and classy.

Whether it's interval training, yoga, home workouts, sports, strength training, circuits, HIIT or other fitness requirements, this app can easily fit in and accelerate your progress. Time your repetitions, forget the count and focus on your moves.

A must-have countdown timer app for exercise enthusiasts, athletes, trainers, coaches and all the fitness freaks who are passionate about exercising.


☆ 2nd Prize Winner of the Google Fit Developer Challenge 2015 ☆
And it has grown more powerful since then...


Features:

• Interval Timer + Counter combined (iCount Timer)
• Save timers/counters as presets organised by activities
• Large clear display with progress bar
• Audio cues
• Smart lock to lock timer screen

• Android wear app
+ Presets from the handheld app are available for you on Android Wear organised by activities.
+ Get vibration alerts on your Android Wear during start/rest of intervals.
+ Supports ambient mode which saves battery
+ Note: Android Wear app requires paired device to run


Pro version features:

• No Ads
• Extended slider limits
• Input custom seconds / counts and repeat interval (rounds)
• Save upto 40 presets
• 5 different themes
• Various choices for alert sounds such as boxing bell etc
• Different counter modes
• Custom start delay
• Calories estimation**
• Google Fit integration
• Landscape mode

** The estimated calories are approximate Calories burned for any selected activity based on the MET (Metabolic Equivalent) values for the activity and personal details of individual. Actual energy expenditure may vary.
