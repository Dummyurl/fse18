Description

Take a moment and think about your last family vacation, road trip, or weekend getaway. What memories do you have? How were they captured? Do you remember what you saw or where you went? Over time you may have posted a few of those memories on social media, but can you even find those posts now?

Today, The Traveler is there to help capture and organize those memories, no matter where your travels may take you. With recorded paths using GPS and Google Maps, the app follows your every step. Captured photos, videos, sketches, audio clips and location markers are geotagged along the path with additional options for notes and place information. Combined with a "guide nearby" feature including more than 9000 guides from 100 countries, fitness activity tracking with Google Fit, and detailed trip journaling capabilities, The Traveler stands apart from other travel journals.

And now, with The Traveler Pass, you can upload all of your memories to the cloud and share them for all your family and friends to see at www.mytravelerapp.com. For pricing information, please visit www.mytravelerapp.com/home/pricing. Travelers who created trips before August 31 will receive a 50% discount on all your Traveler Pass purchases!

For Samsung Galaxy Note devices, The Traveler includes support for the S Pen to provide a sketching and drawing canvas for digital sketchbook capabilities.

Features include:
* Sync your trips, paths and media to the cloud with an in-app purchase of a Traveler Pass;
* Capture photos with audio notes, videos and audio clips on your trip and a marker will be added to the map where they were created;
* Start, pause or stop a new recording, or add a marker from your connected Android Wear device;
* Sync fitness activities as you travel with Google Fit;
* View charts depicting your fitness activities;
* Record paths with high accuracy or balanced location settings to preserve your battery life;
* Share your entire trip with friends and family using Google Drive sync;
* Share media with friends and family using your favorite social media apps;
* View over 9000 guides in 100 countries and 2300+ destinations around the world, provided by GuideAdvisor.com;
* Add your favorite guides to your trip for easy access as you are traveling;
* Export paths to Google Drive;
* Export paths to a file and import using your favorite map app or software;
* Work offline to record your trips no matter where your travels take you;
* Sketch and draw with a variety of brushes, tools and colors using the S Pen.

Ball State University students, faculty and staff can connect their university account for additional features, including:
* Ability to sync all content (trips, paths and media) to a university-based server for archival and transferring to other devices;
* Invite other classmates and faculty to join a trip;
* Share photos and other media assets between travelers;
* Invite friends and family to view your travels on the The Traveler web site - https://apps.bsu.edu/traveler

The Traveler is based on a mobile app developed at Ball State University.
