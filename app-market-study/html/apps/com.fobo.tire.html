Description

World's First All Bluetooth Smart Tire Pressure Monitoring System

Save Life, Save Tire, Save Fuel...

Key Features:

- 24x7 monitoring of your car tire pressure
- Flat tire alert even when you are away from the car
- 3 level smart alert
- Monitors up to 20 cars with one app
- Share FOBO Tire with up to 100 users via simple app authorization

- DIY install in 5 minutes
- Easy tire rotation procedure
- Easy tire sensor replacement procedure
- Anti-theft features
