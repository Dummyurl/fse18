Description

MyAV is a TV remote control app; perfect if you've lost your remote.
Works over Wi-Fi for certain Smart TVs: Sony, Panasonic, Samsung, Sharp & LG and even more makes of TVs if your device has an infrared (IR) blaster.

Additionally, MyAV can also control a range of other products. Other controllable devices include Blu-ray players, set-top boxes, cable & sat and home cinema receivers.
Dish, Roku, Xfinity, DirecTV, Sky+HD (UK)

If you have IR on your Android device then this app can control even more devices.

A full compatibility list can be found on our site:
http://myav.co.uk/compatiblity.htm

This is a free 7 day trial version of the main MyAV app
