KM Clock, Battery widgets and clock watch face for Android Wear

Free widgets for Android devices:
★ Analog clock widget / Analog watch face for Android wea rdevices;

* More Analog watch faces in app "KM Watch faces / Clock Widgets" https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mikhaylov.kolesov.clockwidgets

★ Digital clock widget;
★ Battery widget (battery charge) (four colors for the battery);
★ Switch widget.

Features of the analog clock widget:
★ Hands and faces of analog clocks were created entirely from clay;
★ Customizable analog clock: have it with or without the second hand.

Features of the digital clock widget:
★ Customizable frame color;
★ Customizable digits on display.

Features of the switches widget:
★ Airplane Mode;
★ Bluetooth;
★ Sync;
★ Wi-Fi;
★ GPS;
★ Sound.

Especially for fans of Plasticine Live wallpapers;

Now you can have widgets for turn on/off the: bluetooth, GPS, Sound, Airplane mode by using The KM Switch widget.
Now you can have widgets for monitoring state of the battery by using the KM Battery widget.
Now you can have clock - just use KM Clock widget.
