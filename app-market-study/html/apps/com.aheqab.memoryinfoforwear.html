Description

A small app for your Android Wear device that provides information about

Internal Memory (Available and Total)

RAM (Available and Total)

CPU Usage


To switch to details mode, click on one of the icons on the main screen,

Please note: This app is only for Android Wear SmartWatches.

It supports both Squared (LG G Watch, Samsung Gear Live...) and Round (Moto 360...) watches.
