Description

The FNB Banking App embraces the future of help – helping people to help themselves.
Managing your financial life has never been this easy with the ability to transact, borrow, invest, protect and communicate all in one place.
Download the App to see for yourself.

Select the Help icon on your home screen to see answers to frequently asked questions or select FNB TV to view video tutorials.
_____________________________________________

FNB Pay - Supported on NFC enabled devices with KitKat 4.4 or above
Fingerprint ID - Supported on devices with a Fingerprint sensor running Android 6.0
Watch App - Only available on Android Wear supported devices
Secure Chat - Only available to Premier, Private Clients and Private Wealth
