Description

Display your favourite stock on your watch. This is not an app for your wear device. It is a watchface with your stock data on your wrist. Just look at your watch to see the current financial situation of your stock.

Open the watchface configuration with the Android Wear App.

This watchface is for finance and stock market informations.

Feel free to fill out my Google Forms http://goo.gl/forms/7p3XKYL3je to request new features.

Some symbol samples:
GOOG (Google nasdaq)
AAPL (Apple nasdaq)
^NDX (Nasdaq-100)
BMW.DE (BMW Xetra)

Find more stock symbols on http://finance.yahoo.com/
