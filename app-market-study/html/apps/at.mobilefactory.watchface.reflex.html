Description

Android Wear Watch Face REFLEX
Nice sun reflexions on your watchface, looks like a premium Rolex, Rado or Breitling. Modern design for everyday usage - pure unisex luxury watch - the best watch on the app store for men & women. Simulates sun reflexion so it appears as a real watch like a Omega + nice animations! Round or Rectangle the Watch supports both. BurnIn protection for OLED screens Change settings on the watch or in the companion app.


customization
+ Now you can save up your battery with the ambient brightness setting!
+ Show / Hide Date, Watch Name
+ Reflexion Animation (with the sensors on your watch)
+ Hand Animation (every tick is animated and looks gorgeous)

compatible devices:
Motorola Moto360, LG G Watch, LG G WATCH R, Samsung Gear LIVE, SmartWatch 3, ASUS ZenWatch, Sony 3 Android wear watches
The App will work with ANY smartwatch that runs Android Wear!

install:
After installing the app on your phone, the app automatically installs itself on the watch
Long press on the watchface clock, then select REFLEX or use the companion app.

Suggestions to improve the watch? Let us know by contacting us and we'll do our best.


Currently our Android wear watchfaces app can be used on the Watch R, LG G Watch, Sony, Moto 360 & ASUS Zenwatch, Samsung Gear Live, LG G and all other Android Wear devices. Premium luxary clock like Rolex Breitling Rado Pujie or Omega
