Description

See tennis like never before.

This app is for SONY'S Smart Tennis Sensor "SSE-TN1".
As of April 2016, over 140 compatible rackets are available from major racket brands (Wilson, Head, Prince, and Yonex).
By using the Smart Tennis Sensor and this app, you can easily check your data on the tennis court like never before, such as ball impact position, ball rotation, ball speed, swing speed, etc.
You can also play back recorded video and your play data at the same time using the app on your smartphone.
Your play data can be shared through SNS with fellow tennis players.
Turn your game on!

Alexis Moussine-Pouchkine January 29, 2016
Overall good design, large fonts and UI as it should be. Orientation is killing me: everything is in portait except for the live mode video which is a pain when using a stand (and even with portrait or landscape, it should rotate 180deg if the devices is oriented that way). My biggest gripe is that there seems to be no ability to explicitly start/stop a "session" to split out different parts of trainings or different players (the zooming feature does compensate for the lack of this feature). Another obvious but worth mentioning limitation is that it will count all your shots, including shots hit in the net (serves for instance) or balls out by 2 meters. This is why the live video mode is so useful, assuming you place the camera/tablet behind the player to see where the ball is going. I don't like the requirement for users to create a Sony account before they can use the app/device. It should be opt-in assuming people want to backup or share. At the end of the day this collects great data, offers great UI but you'll have to be able to interpret the data or get help from a pro to really improve your game.
