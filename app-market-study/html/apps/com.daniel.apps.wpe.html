Description

A launcher for an app which lets you dial a number, call a contact form your contacts or recent list on your phone. All your phone contacts and call logs/history will be accessible on your watch.

If watch doesn't have speakers or a microphone, call will be placed on phone otherwise, it will be placed on watch.

Works best with headphones or wireless/Bluetooth headset, as you can dial from your watch and talk through your phone for best quality.
