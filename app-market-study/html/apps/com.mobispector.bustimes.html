Description

* Over 3,500,000 downloads on all platforms!

London Bus Timing Countdown tells you when your next bus will arrive at any bus stop in London using data directly from TfL.

New *Look and Feel*
New *My Buses* - See Arrival times for all buses you take on one screen.
New *Improved Journey Planner*
New *Oyster Balance*
New *Updated Android Wear support*

Features:
* Instant Live Bus times for Transport for London (TFL) bus stops showing when the next buses will arrive
* Finds bus stops near you using your Android phone's GPS/Location Services
* View nearest stops on a map or list
* Automatic Refresh of Bus Times Countdown so you'll always have the most up to date information
* Search for bus stops by postcode or bus stop name
* Save your favourite bus stops in your Favourites for quick access
* See your recent visited stops
* Journey Planner shows you how to reach anywhere on the TfL Network using Buses, Tube, Trains etc.
* My Buses puts all your bus routes onto one screen. Giving you bus times at a glance.
* View your Oyster Balance

If you like what we're doing, leave us a 5 star rating :)

Please be aware that this app requires an internet connection and Location services for full functionality.

* This app uses the Bus countdown feed from TFL, and they occasionally do maintenance in which case the application will show incomplete results.
