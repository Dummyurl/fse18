Description

What can you do with the TIAA app?

Glad you asked. View your retirement plan balances and rates of return on the go.
We offer brokerage trading for equities, ETFs, and mutual funds. You can also fund your brokerage and TIAA Personal Portfolio account without having to log into your home computer. View your tax statements anytime, anywhere - right from the palm of your hand. Easily transfer funds, check pending transactions, share a file with TIAA, view your checking account and more! 

TIAA is proud to offer ease and convenience with just a single swipe or tap of your smartphone. Exciting new features are at hand. Download the TIAA app today.

Log in - Quickly and securely with Fingerprint Authentication.

Banking - Pay your bill or deposit your check right from your phone. *

Brokerage trading - Buy and sell equities, ETFs and mutual funds while on the go.  

Transfer funds - Move money from one bank account to another using your finger.

Pending transactions - Monitor your transaction requests.

Peek View - View your total portfolio and balances without logging in.  

Stay on top of your investments - Monitor your account activity, contributions and asset allocation.

Track fund performance - Regardless if it’s your own funds or ones you’re just watching.

Register for account access - easily manage your portfolio digitally with just a few clicks.

Contact TIAA - Find an ATM, contact your advisor, share a file, locate offices, and send a message.

Your TIAA mobile app is here to help you reach your financial goals. TIAA Founder Andrew Carnegie established that dedication almost 100 years ago. That same commitment is evident today.
