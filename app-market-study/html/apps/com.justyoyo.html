Description

Yoyo Wallet is mobile payment and rewards rolled into one: collect points, stamps and vouchers for every single pound or cent spent.

Free, fast, and rewarding - there’s a reason why Yoyo Wallet is Europe’s fastest-growing mobile payment app.

Make payment more rewarding and upgrade your wallet today.

INSTANT PAYMENT:
All you need is your mobile phone - searching for credit cards, counting change and losing cash are now a thing of the past.
Scan and Go - simply scan your mobile phone at the checkout for fast, secure payment.
Keep track of your spending - no more paper receipts filling out your wallet. Save time, save the environment!

GREAT REWARDS:
Add value to every transaction - instantly earn loyalty points and use them to claim the things you love at a time that suits you.
Never miss out on offers or rewards again - you’ll be notified automatically when a reward is waiting for you.
Get more of what you love - whether it’s a free coffee or a discount burrito, we let you choose the rewards.
Get ahead with Pre-Buy - benefit from exclusive offers and buy tickets, food and drink in-app.

SECURITY
Peace of mind - Yoyo Wallet never holds on to your money or your payment details. Pay with ease, pay with confidence.
Mix it up - our QR code payment technology is randomised every few seconds to ensure that codes cannot be duplicated for fraudulent use.
No code, no entry - Yoyo Wallet users are able to set a passcode. This adds a layer of security to ensure that you are the only one making payments from your account.

Any questions? Don’t be shy, contact us at support@yoyowallet.com
