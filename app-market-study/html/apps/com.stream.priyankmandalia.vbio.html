Description

Stream movies & songs from your phone or tablet to HDTV using Google Chromecast and control it using your phone or Android Wear watch.

★ Featured on AppsZoom and AndroidTapp★

Vbio App Highlights:

1) Now no need to connect your phone to your HDTV using HDMI or USB cable. Stream your movies, personal videos and songs from phone or tablet to your HDTV using Google Chromecast. Just select your phone from the device selection screen and start streaming your content.

2) App will also show new and featured movies which are running in theaters or going to release, so that you can play new trailers directly on TV. It will show ratings given to those movies and description of it. You can even add movies to your watch-list.

3) You can also stream movies from your PC (Watch demo: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4K0zUNsmnlM ). For that you just need to install "PS3 Media Server" on your PC which is available for free to download (http://www.ps3mediaserver.org/). Then add your network IP address in settings and add movies folder in it. Then you will see your PC media server on list of available devices on Vbio device selection screen. Just select it start streaming from your PC. (Note:It works perfectly if videos and movies are in default "Videos" folder of the PC and should be in .MP4 format.)

4) User can even control the videos or songs playback using Android Wear smartwatch (Watch Demo: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I3BQgi-BPWI ).

*Note:
Wait for 5-10 seconds if Chromecast button does't appear instantly.

*Instruction for creating playlist:

To create playlist, first select your phone or tablet from device selection screen and click on playlist from the top bar. Then from the top bar click on "Add Playlist" and create one. You can create as many playlist as you want.

To add videos to playlist, just long-press videos and start adding them to you preferred playlist.
