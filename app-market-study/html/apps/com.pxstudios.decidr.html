Description

If you're indecisive and have trouble deciding whether you should go to bed or keep looking at cat videos all night, choosing between pizza or Chinese for dinner, figuring out who's turn it is to get the next round... or pretty much any thing for that matter... Then look no further, Decidr is the app for you!

Decidr includes the following tools and games to help you make the best decision!

★ Yes or No
★ Truth or Dare
★ Heads or Tails
★ Spin the Bottle
★ Magic 8 Ball

Simply flick up on the screen and you'll have your answer. Decidr is also fully compatible with Android Wear so you can make quick decisions on the go!
