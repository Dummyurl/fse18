Description

Gett is a global ride sharing app built on a simple idea - if you treat drivers better, they will treat riders better.

Gett is available in more than 100 cities across Europe, including London, Moscow, and Tel Aviv. Download Gett today to start riding with the best rated drivers in Russia, the UK and Israel.

In the US, Gett acquired Juno to provide the highest quality service. Download the Juno app today to start riding with the best rated drivers in NYC!

We are here for you, with 24/7 support.
In the US, call Juno at + (844) JUNO USA (844-586-6872) or email us at help@gojuno.com.
