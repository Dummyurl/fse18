Gray Strickland
Best so far, but... This is the first timer/stopwatch app that I've been able to find that lets you start a simple timer on your watch AND SEE STATUS as it runs. Unfortunately, it keeps your watch on to this app constantly and if you dismiss it (by covering hte watch face with your palm), IT STOPS. Not only does it stop, but it doesn't even pickup where it left off. I'll be the first to admit that I don't know if this behavior is a flaw in the app or in the Android Wear OS, but either way it sucks. What if i need to run a three hour countdown timer (which is pretty common for BBQ smokers)? I shouldn't have to have this app running down my watch battery by running the screen on full the whole time, nor should I have to miss interim notifications from other apps nor the ability to JUST SEE MY NORMAL WATCH FACE. What we need is a timer (and stopwatch) that we can start and stop from the watch, see status (running time) on demand, but which will drop to the background and CONTINUE RUNNING.
