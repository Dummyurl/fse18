Description

Dot On the Run – Dot Rush
The Dot On the Run is a simple, interesting & fun Circle Dot Run game that will make your play for hours.

How to Play Dot On the Run / Circle Dot Run: -
Circle Dot Run game play is so simple. Tap the screen to control your white ball. Hold your finger on screen to speed up your ball to dodge the rush of other balls. You have to control your white ball into the circle so that do not impact with other balls on other circle. The game becomes more difficult when more balls on other circle & you have to dodge all of them.
You will get one score when you finish a round of circle without touching the blue ball.

A beautiful interface with exciting sound track, we hope you love this Dot On the Run / Dot Rush game.

Game features : -
Two Player game mode
Leaderboard & achievements
Share your high score on social sites & challenge your friends.

Dot Rush game is definitely a good choice for those who like challenging games. This game is completely free so download now & enjoy the game Circle Dot Rush.
