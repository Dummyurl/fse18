Description

• Keep it simple

Create a note in one click, it will automatically be saved on your device.
Access all your notes at one glance.
Archive or delete notes when you are done with it.

• Make it personal

Change the color of notes to highlight what matters.
Sort notes by date created, date modified or by color.

• Google account

Sign in with your Google account to sync your notes.

• Android Wear

Access all your notes from your wearable.

• Other features

Add reminders and receive notifications.
Add widget to home screen.
Share notes with other apps.
Open a link directly from a note.

• Free

This app is free and contains no advertising.
