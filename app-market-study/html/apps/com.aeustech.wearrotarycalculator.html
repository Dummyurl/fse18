Description

Designed exclusively for Android Wear, Wear Rotary Calculator takes a bold new step in bringing a time-tested utility app into the 21st century, all on the comfort of your own wrist.


- Beautifully redesigned interface
- Tap Center to bring up Basic Operations
- Press "Mode Toggle button" to use Advanced Mode (In-App Purchase)
- Swipe Up to switch between Radian and Degree Mode
- Swipe Right to Exit


COMPATIBLE ANDROID WEAR DEVICES:
Samsung Gear Live, LG G Watch, LG G Watch R, Sony Smartwatch 3, Asus ZenWatch and/or Moto 360 running Android 4.3 or higher
