Description

The ever cheerful Carla and her grumpy octopus Schnuu are inseparable. With this watch face you will gain a first insight into the exciting daily routine of the two.

Illustrated by the incomparable Melanie Schober, these two chibi figures will not just make Manga Fans smile.

Carla & Schnuu Lite includes:
- a watch face for smart watches (Android Wear)
- a homescreen widget for smart phones
