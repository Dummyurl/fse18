Description

***New version supports Moto 360 just released!

**As this is still in beta, we appreciate comments and feedback to help make this app better. Thank you for the feedback. We've made changes to the app in response to your comments. With the latest version, it now supports all Android smart watches including Moto 360, LG G, and Samsung Gear Live.

Introducing the first card game app for Android smart watches, Blackjack Wear. Play Vegas style black jack from the watch with swipe gestures.

Blackjack Wear is compatible with the latest version of Android wear for all smart watches including LG G Watch, and Samsung Gear Live.

Beta Features Include:
Play with easy to use swipe gestures
--Swipe down to Hit
--Swipe up to Stay
Change your blackjack table from the "options" menu
Lessen or Increase your bet amounts
Double Down

Upcoming Features:
Improved UI
Split Cards
Gesture/Click Option

There's a new way to play this classic card game...on your watch! If you like playing Blackjack aka twenty-one on your phone, you'll love playing Blackjack on your wearable Android smart watch: LG G Watch, Moto 360, and Samsung Gear Live.
