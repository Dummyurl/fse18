Description

Party like it's 2999 with a colorful glowing display on your phone, tablet or at your wrist that dynamically changes with any ambient sound or music, in real-time.

This version contains 10 renderers by default but you can individually purchase more renderers, pack of renderers, everything or directly take the full version which contains all different music visualizers unlocked at http://goo.gl/NWY02s

Round and square screens are supported for Wear.


Concern about the energy impact?
Our tests showed that it can easily run continuously for more than 3 hours and a half on a smartwatch and far more on a regular device. Most consumed energy is in fact taken by the screen, not by the app itself.

About RECORD_AUDIO permission
Needed only to acquire audio from the microphone in order to generate the visualization reacting to the sound. Nothing is saved at all.
