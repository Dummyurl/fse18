What's New

This is the next major release of Rotating Watch Face brining a total revamp of configuration screen on Phone and Watch including the addition of these new options;
The colour of the hours can be chosen from a list of Red, Green, Blue, Cyan and Yellow.
The hours mode can be toggled between 12 and 24 hour.
This release also remembers your settings after reboot and has some performance enhancements for Android M.
