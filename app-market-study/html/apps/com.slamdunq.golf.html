Description

Note :- This app is no longer supported.

Turn your Samsung Gear S2, Gear Fit 2 or Android wear smartwatch into your very own Golf Buddy!

SlamdunQ Golf has been designed to be your golf buddy, be it on the course or the driving range.
Strap on your smartwatch, pair your SlamdunQ app on your phone and you are all set.
We will help keep your score card as you play your Game or help you perfect your swing on the Driving Range.

Gaming Mode (New Feature)

Using GPS technology and motion sensing, we now put the power of 30,000 golf courses right on your wrist.
- 30,000 golf courses across the world
- Yards, Par and HCP on your smartwatch
- GPS based detection of distance to green
- Motion-sending based stroke detection
- Auto scorecard generation

Driving Range Mode:
SlamdunQ golf helps you analyze your swing through bio-mechanics explained clearly via expertly designed parameters.

Sensors detect all the parameters a golf coach would advice for swing analysis and present the data in a concise and informative form on the phone. A state of the art animation shows you exact movement of your swing bringing to you, your very own ‘action replay’.


Supported devices:
1. Samsung gear S2
2. Samsung gear Fit2
2. LG-G and LG-Urbane watches
3. Sony Smartwatch 3
4. Asus ZenWatch
5. Huawei Watch
6. Moto360 2nd Gen
Tested with:
1. Moto 360 - sensors freeze a reset helps occasionally

Please read https://golf.slamdunq.in/SlamdunqGolf/resources/permissions.html to understand what permissions SlamdunQ Golf requires and why.
