Description

Belly is a universal loyalty program that offers one-of-a-kind rewards at the businesses you love, replacing boring "Buy 10, Get 1 Free" punch cards with free stuff you actually want. Belly Members scan their BellyCard or app at the in-store tablet, earning points that are redeemable for rewards unique to each Belly Business.




With Belly, we offer one customer loyalty card to replace the rest. Link the mobile app to your physical BellyCard which can be picked up at any Belly Business. Use the app's "Nearby Places" feature to find locations to Belly when you're out and about on the town. Earn a free cup of coffee at the donut shop down the street. Name the Sandwich of the Month at your local deli. For the first time, you can have your cake and eat it too, or at least have the option of shoving it into your baker's face.

Use your Google+ account to register with Belly! Keep your accounts connected for an easier user experience.

Add the Belly widget to your Homescreen or Lockscreen for instant access! (The Lockscreen widget is only available on devices running Android 4.2+)

Belly when you’re jet setting across the country. Major markets include:
• Chicago
• Austin
• New York
• Washington, D.C.
• Miami
• San Francisco
And more at bellycard.com/locations

Permissions Descriptions
All information collected via the permissions below are used to enhance the app experience and allow us to continue to make it better than the best:

Storage: Used to cache images and other data to make the app load and function better.
Your Location (GPS & Network): Allows us to determine your location so we can find Businesses near you when you only when the app is open.
Network Communications: Used to gather nearby businesses, account information and other data needed for the application to function.
View Network/Wifi State: We can ensure you have an active internet connection before trying to download assets or information.
Your Accounts: Used for Google Cloud Messaging notifications (optional in settings), and for an auto-complete email field on login/registration
Vibrate: Used for Push Notification alert behavior (optional in settings)
Wake Lock: Used for Google Cloud Messaging (push) notifications (optional in settings)
Read Google Services Configurations: Required for the Google Maps v2 API.
Camera: Used ONLY during setup for signing in with a physical Belly Card (optional)

Review our Privacy Policy for additional security questions or feel free to contact us.
