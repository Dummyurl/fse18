Description

Now featuring real-time information for Luas Green Line (Cross City) extension!

For both phones and wearables, Luas at a Glance is the most feature-rich and slickest Luas app for Android. Designed using the latest Material Design guidelines, Luas at a Glance is clean, simple to use and completely free of ads. It's built with you in mind, to make your Dublin trams commute easier and faster.

Features:
• Real-time information for new Luas Green Line (Cross City) extension.

• Never miss your next Luas with incoming tram notifications - just tap the tram you plan to take, choose when you want to be reminded and finish your morning coffee in peace.

• Jump to your regular stops in just two taps using the built-in Favourites feature.

• Quick and easy maps of the entire Dublin Luas network, at the touch of a button.

• Calculate your fare price before you get to the ticket machine using the handy fares calculator.

• Create a widget for your most-frequented stops and get tram information straight to your homescreen. [BETA]

• See the latest Luas news and travel disruptions.

• Cainteoir Gaeilge? Tá Luas at a Glance ar fáil i nGaeilge! - Irish speaker? Luas at a Glance is available in Irish!
(Note: Requires Irish language support on device)

Luas at a Glance is free and open source software and is made available under the GNU General Public License.

Source code, bugs and feature requests:
https://github.com/thecosmicfrog/LuasataGlance

Irish language translations kindly provided by Feidhlim Seoighe.
