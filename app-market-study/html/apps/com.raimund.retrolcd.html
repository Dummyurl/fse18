Description

Travel back to the 90s and get the old LCD Feeling to your wrist.

Many settings like ...
★ custom layout adjustment (Text Position and Size)
★ time format selectable (12 / 24H)
★ date format selectabe (Day-Month / Month-Day) Month as number or short text
★ Weekday
★ Week number
★ Watch Battery Level

★★★★★ more Features are in progress!

This digital watchface is compatible with Android Wear™watches!

Added support for Android Wear 2.0, maybe not stable!!
Please have a look under Companion App Watchface settings
Infotab Version when it ends with an .1 you have the Wear 2.0
face installed.

For Layout corrections please send me an screenshot of your the watchface
with the selected Infotab VERSION thru E-Mail or G+.
