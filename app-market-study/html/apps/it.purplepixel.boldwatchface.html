Description

You will be delighted by Bold's bright colors and smooth animations: let your Android Wear smart watch come to life with this Material Design inspired watch face!

As time passes by the ink will ripple through the watch. When the screen goes off, a gray ink ripple will dim the scene; and colorful ripples of ink will bring it back to life as the screen turns on.

★Features
✓ Analog watch face
✓ Digital watch face with time and date

✓ Material Design color palette: choose your tint and accent color from Azure, Indigo, Pink, Orange, Teal and Perl;
✓ Ripple tint animations on state transactions - from Ambient to Interactive mode and vice versa;
✓ Ink tinting animation as seconds hand;

✓ Optimized for both round and square displays
✓ Support for Ambient mode;
✓ Support for Low Bit mode [great for OLED displays]
✓ Handheld companion app for customization;
✓ Control the watch face Frame Rate from the handheld companion app: if you want to prolong battery life, choose Normal or Acceptable; if your battery is really down and you wanna save as much power as possible (or if you don't mind having not-so-smooth animations!) choose Clumsy
✓ Time format as system default - support for 12 and 24 hours
✓ Support for synchronization with system time format and time zone;

Wait for more features to come!
