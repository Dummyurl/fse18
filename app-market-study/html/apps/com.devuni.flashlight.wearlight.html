Description

Wear Light is a plugin for Tiny Flashlight and depends on it.

Wear Light enables the full display brightness of your Android Wear smart watch. A fast and handy light source. Very convenient when you need to hit the lock in the dark.

Features:
=========
* Turn on the light by saying "Open Light"
* Full screen brightness
* Automatically installed on you Android Wear smart watch
