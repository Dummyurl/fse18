Description

Use Rewardle at your favourite local businesses all across Australia to collect points and redeem rewards. With Rewardle you can also order, prepay and unlock special features and benefits only available to app members.

The Rewardle app allows you to:

• View all your visited favourite local businesses.
• Order ahead before you arrive.
• Mobile payment, no need to carry cash with you.
• Link your Rewardle card, to keep your points, rewards and offers in one place.

We take your security seriously. Your credit card information is never stored by Rewardle or on your device. Your information is stored securely by our payment service provider.

Review our Privacy Policy for additional security question or feel free to contact us at support@rewardle.com.

Learn more about our Android permissions: http://www.rewardle.com/permissions/android
Like us on Facebook: http://www.facebook.com/rewardle
Follow us on Twitter: http://twitter.com/wearerewardle
