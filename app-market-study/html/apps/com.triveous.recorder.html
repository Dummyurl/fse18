Description

Please note: This Voice Recorder cannot record calls of any type

This is not just a voice recorder, it is an intelligent voice recorder. It is the only app that lets you automatically add images of presentation slides, notes, whiteboard sessions and more to a recording to make it richer.


It is also beautiful, reliable and records audio in high quality, that is why every day, thousands of people in over 200 countries and 15 languages trust this app to record their meetings, class lectures, music sessions, voice memos & more.





Why you should try this
★ Enjoy crystal clear audio recording for hours in all conditions on every device.
★ A beautifully simple user experience that’s been refined over years of user research. The experience is further enhanced by the principles of material design.
★ No advertisements, no limits on the recording duration. Audio recording like it should be.
★ Add images of notes, presentation slides and more to the recording to make it richer and watch how this automatically creates a slideshow for you.
★ This makes searching for recordings easier than ever before. Organize your audio recordings with tags like work, important, school and even your location, so finding the right recording is always a breeze.
★ This app auto-saves your recording every minute, so that even if the phone crashes or runs out of battery you should be able to recover it.
★ We know space is always an issue with large recordings. That is why you can compress audio and save to an external SD card



Other features:
★ You can also optionally backup your recordings to Dropbox so that you always have a copy of your recordings.
★ This app makes it really easy to share your sound recordings and voice memos over bluetooth, mms, email & also with third party apps
★ We love android tablets and that's why we've created a great, completely customized experience for tablets.
★ Many many more recording options for the inner geek in us (hi-definition audio settings, sharing to soundcloud, adjusting recording volume with microphone gain)
★ Fully accessible



The pro version of this Voice Recorder allows you to do even more:
- All compression bitrates
- All Sync Settings
- All themes
- Recording on a bluetooth headset
- Customized Pro Widgets
- Change recording source (camcorder microphone)
- Noise Suppression
- Echo Cancellation
- Automatic Gain Control



So why use this app?
This is the the best free voice recorder for businessmen, school & college students, musicians, journalists & people who love to record audio on the go. Use it for
Meetings: It is the best voice recorder to be used as a business meeting recorder. Take photos of presentations, whiteboard sessions or notes during the meeting & they will be synced to the recording.
Lectures: School, college & university students frequently use this app to record lectures. Take images of a whiteboard, notes or pages in a book & you have a virtual class on your phone. Tip: It might also make sense to create a shared dropbox folder for all students in a class, school, college or university & then use this app to share to that.
Music & Sound: This audio recorder is also great at recording sounds of different musical instruments like the guitar, flute etc. so people love to use it as a dedicated sound recorder.
