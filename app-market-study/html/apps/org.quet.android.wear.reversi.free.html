Description

The classic strategy board game for Wear.

Square and round screens are supported.

FEATURES
• One player (human vs CPU)
• Two players (human vs human)
• Zero player (CPU vs CPU)
• Grid sizes (4x4, 6x6 and 8x8)
• Variable difficulty
• Naive AI (negascout)

HOW TO USE IT
• At start, swipe to the right to select the desired mode (one player, two players, etc.)
• In game, swipe up to view the score. Swipe up again to restart or select another mode
• Simple tap to drop a disc
• Double tap to zoom the board
• Long press the screen to quit
