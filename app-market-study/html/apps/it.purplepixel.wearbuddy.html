Description

WearBuddy let your Android Wear device monitor and control its essential companion: your phone!
Please note: this is the very first release: more features to come soon!

Features:
- Get a contextual notification on your wearable device when your phone is out of range. Never forget it on the table again!
- Get a contextual notification on your Android Wear device stream when your phone battery is running low.
- Find my phone: never miss it under the sofa again!
- Stop the phone ringing when you finally find it: tap on the notification or swipe it away to make it stop.
- Stop your phone ringing directly from your watch notification stream: just tap on the card or swipe it away
- Open the handheld app to check the connection with your Android Wear device

Of course, an Android Wear device is needed!
