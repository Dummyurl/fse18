Description

A+ Notes lets you create, edit and save notes. A+ Notes gives you a quick and easy note taking experience. It's not only simple and easy to use but also comes with many features:

-Send notes to Android Wear
-Copy, paste and edit entries
-Customizable background and font(bold,underline,font color)
-Insert pictures from camera and gallery
-Share and sort
-Folders to group notes
-Maths symbols
-Transferable to external storage(sd card)
-Option for hiding toolbar for simple UI experience
