Description

Dublin Bikes is a tool allowing you to view the status of bike stations of Dublin and suburbs.

Through the map, view closest stations to your location, follow the number of bikes and the numbers of available slots.

Find a station easily with adress or name

You can add favorite and use the dedicated widget, or check on your Android Wear watch !

Feel free to send me your suggestions or problems, thanks to the dedicated tab in the app.
