Description

Please try the demo version before buying
Do you have an Android Wear device?
Did your ever wish you didn't have to shout to it in public to reply a message?
Do you prefer replying from your watch with keyboards or quick replies?


This is the app for you!


Your freedom is just a few taps away. Get the app, enable it and you are all set!
This app currently works with:
- WhatsApp
- Hangouts
- Gmail
- Telegram
- Facebook Messenger
- Threema
- Viber
- Inbox by Gmail
- Line
- CloudMagic
- KakaoTalk


And will generate a full notification for those apps with:
- Keyboard support
- Customizable quick replies
- Customizable vibration pattern
- Sound support
- Picture viewing (not all apps supported)
- Voice note playback (WhatsApp only)


No root or any modification needed!


Please note: Remember to block your apps from the Android Wear app, or you will get double notifications. (QFW may appear below the original notification)


Setup instructions: https://youtu.be/5O9mv3TurbQ
