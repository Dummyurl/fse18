Description

Need to catch a train?
In a hurry?
Worried you'll be late?

Search on your mobile for specific train departures from any UK station and use the real time notifications to keep you up to date with its status.

A dedicated service tracking app on your android wear reduce distractions, allowing your to cycle, drive walk or run to your preferred station without having to keep checking your mobile

No more reaching the station only to find the train you rushed to catch is running late; Track My Train keeps you up to date with any service's progress.


There is a beta version of the app available with newer features, sign up here: https://play.google.com/apps/testing/uk.co.rossbeazley.trackmytrain.android
