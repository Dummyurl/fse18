Description

Take great photos and videos the easy and natural way remotely!
This is the official Sony Camera remote control application for your Sony SmartWatch 3.

With this app you can check the angle and control the shutter of your SONY Action Cam using your SmartWatch 3. You can also start and stop videos, capture photos using our timer function selected Action Cam* and check the pictures taken remotely in the preview screen mode.

*Depends on capability of timer function of Action Cam

To install this app Android Wear OS 5.1.1 or higher is required on your SmartWatch 3.

Works with SONY Action cam: HDR-AS50, FDR-X1000V, HDR-AS200V, HDR-AS100V, HDR-AZ1, HDR-AS30V, HDR-AS20

If you want to develop apps or app extensions for innovative Sony accessories, go to
www.sonymobile.com/developer

Enjoy!
